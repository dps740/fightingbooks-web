# FightingBooks Marketing Plan

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Parents of children ages 6-12
- **Secondary:** Teachers, librarians, homeschool families
- **Psychographics:** Value education + entertainment, willing to pay for quality kids content

---

## Marketing Channels & Budget (Year 1)

### 1. SEO & Content Marketing - $0-100/year
- **Blog posts** (already have 16 battle guides)
- **Target keywords:** "who would win", "animal battles for kids", "educational animal books"
- **Cost:** Time only (or $100 for SEO tools like Ubersuggest)
- **Expected:** 500-2,000 organic visitors/month by month 6

### 2. Social Media (Organic) - $0
- **Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram, Facebook
- **Content:** Battle clips, fun facts, "who would win?" polls
- **Frequency:** 3-5 posts/week
- **Cost:** Time only
- **Expected:** 1,000-5,000 followers by month 6

### 3. Social Media (Paid) - $500-1,500/year
- **Platforms:** Facebook/Instagram ads targeting parents
- **Budget:** $50-150/month
- **Targeting:** Parents, ages 28-45, interests in kids education, books
- **Expected CPA:** $2-5 per free signup, $20-50 per paid conversion
- **Expected:** 100-300 paid conversions/year

### 4. Influencer Partnerships - $200-500/year
- **Target:** Parenting bloggers, homeschool influencers, teacher TikTokers
- **Approach:** Free Ultimate access + affiliate commission (20%)
- **Budget:** $200-500 for micro-influencer posts
- **Expected:** 50-200 conversions from influencer referrals

### 5. School & Library Outreach - $100-200/year
- **Approach:** Free trials for classrooms, library partnerships
- **Materials:** Flyers, teacher guides (digital, low cost)
- **Cost:** $100-200 for printed materials if needed
- **Expected:** Word-of-mouth, long-tail growth

### 6. Product Hunt / Indie Launch - $0
- **One-time:** Launch on Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, relevant subreddits
- **Cost:** Time only
- **Expected:** 500-2,000 visitors on launch day, press coverage

### 7. Email Marketing - $0-200/year
- **Platform:** Free tier of Mailchimp/Buttondown
- **Content:** New animal announcements, battle of the week
- **Cost:** $0 until 500+ subscribers, then ~$20/month
- **Expected:** 10-20% email â†’ paid conversion rate

## Year 1 Marketing Timeline

**Months 1-2: Foundation**
- Finalize product, fix bugs
- Set up social accounts
- Write 5+ SEO blog posts
- Create social content templates

**Month 3: Soft Launch**
- Product Hunt launch
- Reddit posts (r/parenting, r/homeschool, r/teachers)
- Begin organic social posting

**Months 4-6: Paid Acquisition**
- Start Facebook/Instagram ads ($50-100/month)
- Reach out to 10-20 micro-influencers
- School outreach begins

**Months 7-12: Scale What Works**
- Double down on best-performing channels
- Increase ad spend if ROI positive
- Build email list for retention
