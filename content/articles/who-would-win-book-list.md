## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many Who Would Win books are there?
As of 2026, there are **35+ books** in the series, including one-on-one battles and multi-animal rumble books. Jerry Pallotta continues to release new titles each year.

### What reading level are Who Would Win books?
Who Would Win books are written for **grades 1-4** with Lexile levels between 560-850. They're perfect for independent reading in grades 2-3 or read-alouds for younger children.

### What age is Who Would Win appropriate for?
The books are designed for children ages **6-10**. The battles are exciting but not graphic — focused on animal facts rather than violence.

### What order should I read Who Would Win books?
There's no required reading order — each book is standalone. We recommend starting with **Lion vs Tiger** or **Polar Bear vs Grizzly Bear** as they feature familiar animals.

### Who writes the Who Would Win books?
**Jerry Pallotta** writes the Who Would Win series. He's also known for his alphabet books. **Rob Bolster** illustrates the series.

### Are there more Who Would Win books coming?
Yes! Jerry Pallotta releases several new Who Would Win books each year. Check his official website or Scholastic for announcements.
