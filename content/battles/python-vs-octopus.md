### The Python

The Green Python, also known scientifically as *Morelia viridis*, is a magnificent snake that calls the tropical rainforests of New Guinea and Australia its home. **Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approx. 30 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 13 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can strike at speeds of 3 mph!
  
**Weapons:**

- Sharp teeth
- Camouflage

**Fighting Style:**
The Green Python relies on its incredible strength and stealth to ambush prey. It typically hunts solo, using its sharp eyesight and ability to remain motionless for long periods. When it strikes, it wraps around its victim, constricting with immense force until the prey is subdued.

### The Octopus

The Common Octopus, scientifically known as *Octopus vulgaris*, is a fascinating creature that inhabits oceans and seas around the world. With its soft body and eight flexible arms, this intelligent mollusk is renowned for its problem-solving abilities and escape artistry.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approx. 15 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 3.5 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can jet through water at 25 mph!

**Weapons:**
- Beak (1 inch)
- Ink cloud
- Camouflage

**Fighting Style:**
The Common Octopus is an agile fighter that uses its quick reflexes and intelligence to outmaneuver opponents. It can squirt a cloud of ink to escape danger and utilizes its remarkable camouflage skills to blend in with its surroundings. When it strikes, it uses its beak to deliver a powerful bite, often targeting the soft parts of its prey.
