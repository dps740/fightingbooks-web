## Meet the Fighters

### The Hyena

The spotted hyena, known scientifically as *Crocuta crocuta*, roams the savannas, grasslands, and woodlands of Africa. Often misunderstood, these clever hunters have a reputation for being scavengers, but they are skilled predators in their own right!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 90 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder and 5.5 to 6.5 feet long! That's about as long as a small sedan!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 37 miles per hour! That's faster than a speeding car in a school zone!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,100 PSI!
- Sharp claws
- Strong legs for running

**Fighting Style:**
Hyenas are known for their pack behavior, often hunting in groups called clans, which can contain up to 80 members! They use teamwork and strategy to outsmart their prey, employing tactics like ambushes and coordinated attacks. When alone, they rely on their speed and powerful jaws to take down smaller animals.

### The Stegosaurus

Meet the mighty stegosaurus, or *Stegosaurus ungulatus*, a giant herbivore that once roamed the dry, open woodlands and plains of the Jurassic period. With its distinctive back plates and spiked tail, this dinosaur is a true marvel of prehistoric evolution!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 5 tons (approximately 10,000 pounds)
- **Length / Height:** About 30 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 5 miles per hour! That's like a slow jog!

**Weapons:**
- Tail spikes (up to 2 feet long)
- Plates on back
- Strong legs

**Fighting Style:**
Stegosaurus primarily relied on its impressive defenses rather than aggression. When threatened, it could swing its spiked tail, known as a "thagomizer," to ward off attackers. Its massive size and armored plates provided a solid defense against predators, making it a tough opponent in any encounter.
