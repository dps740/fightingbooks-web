### The Brachiosaurus

The Brachiosaurus, or *Brachiosaurus altithorax*, was one of the largest dinosaurs to ever walk the Earth, thriving in the lush forests and floodplains of the late Jurassic and early Cretaceous periods. With its long front legs and immense size, it was designed to reach the treetops, munching on leaves and vegetation that many other dinosaurs couldn’t access.
