### The Stegosaurus

The Stegosaurus, scientifically named *Stegosaurus ungulatus*, is one of the most iconic dinosaurs that roamed the Earth during the Jurassic period. This magnificent creature inhabited dry, open woodlands and plains, munching on plants, leaves, and ferns. With its distinct double row of large, bony plates along its back and a tail equipped with sharp spikes, the Stegosaurus was a formidable herbivore in its own right.
