## Meet the Fighters

### The Gorilla

Gorillas are the largest primates on Earth, residing in the tropical forests of Africa. These gentle giants are known for their impressive strength and social behavior, living in groups called troops. Despite their intimidating appearance, gorillas are herbivores, munching on more than 100 types of plants, including leaves, stems, and fruit.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 400 pounds!
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet tall and 12 feet long when standing on all fours! That's as tall as a door!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding car in a school zone!

**Weapons:**
- Strong arms with a reach of 8 feet
- Powerful jaws
- Intimidating chest thump

**Fighting Style:**
Gorillas are known for their brute strength and powerful techniques. They often engage in direct confrontations, relying on their massive size and powerful limbs to intimidate rivals. When threatened, a gorilla might thump its chest to assert dominance before charging at an opponent, using their long arms and strength to grapple and overpower.

### The Griffin

The griffin is a legendary creature that combines the majesty of an eagle and the strength of a lion. With its lion’s body and eagle’s head and wings, this mythical beast is often associated with strength, courage, and protection. Griffins are said to inhabit mountains and ancient ruins, guarding treasures and fiercely defending their territory.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** (approx.) 600 pounds
- **Length / Height:** About 13 feet long and 6 feet tall at the shoulder! That's like a small car!
- **Top Speed:** Can fly up to 30 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding car!

**Weapons:**
- Sharp claws (up to 6 inches long)
- Powerful beak
- Talons for gripping

**Fighting Style:**
Griffins are aerial hunters, using their impressive wings to soar above their prey. They attack from the sky, swooping down with incredible speed to catch their target off guard. With sharp claws and a powerful beak, they can deliver devastating strikes. If outmatched, a griffin can take to the skies, using its agility to evade ground-based attacks.
