The stegosaurus, scientifically known as *Stegosaurus ungulatus*, roamed the Earth during the late Jurassic period, thriving in dry, open woodlands and plains. This gigantic herbivore is famous for its distinctive back plates and spiked tail, which set it apart from other dinosaurs. Though it may seem slow and lumbering, the stegosaurus was a formidable opponent in its own right.
