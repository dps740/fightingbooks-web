The king cobra, or *Ophiophagus hannah*, is a master of stealth and agility, found in the tropical forests and grasslands of Southeast Asia. As the longest venomous snake in the world, it can grow up to 18 feet long and is known for its ability to take down prey much larger than itself.

- **Weight:** Up to 20 pounds, like a small dog!

**Fighting Style:**
King cobras are ambush predators, often coiling and waiting for the right moment to strike. Their venom is incredibly potent, capable of paralyzing and killing their prey within minutes. When threatened, a king cobra can rear up and spread its hood to appear larger and more intimidating, using its speed and agility to evade attacks.
