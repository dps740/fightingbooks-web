## Meet the Fighters

### The T-Rex

The Tyrannosaurus Rex, often simply known as T-Rex, is one of the largest and most fearsome carnivorous dinosaurs to roam the Earth. This magnificent beast lived around 68 to 66 million years ago in what is now North America. Known for its massive size and powerful bite, the T-Rex has earned its reputation as the ultimate predator of its time.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 9 tons
- **Length / Height:** 40 feet long / 12 feet tall
- **Top Speed:** 12 mph
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 8,000 pounds of bite force

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
