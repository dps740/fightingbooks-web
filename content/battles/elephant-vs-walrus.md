### The Walrus

The walrus (Odobenus rosmarus) is a fascinating marine mammal found in the icy Arctic and sub-Arctic regions. With their distinctive tusks and blubbery bodies, walruses are equipped for life in the cold and often gather in large social groups. They are primarily carnivores, feasting on clams and other sea creatures.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 2,000 pounds!
- **Length / Height:** Up to 12 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can swim up to 35 mph! That's like a speeding car on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Tusks up to 3 feet long!

- Massive body weight

**Fighting Style:**
Walruses rely on their tusks and blubbery bodies in fights. They can be quite aggressive when threatened, using their tusks to jab at attackers while also utilizing their weight to push opponents around. They often fight in groups, which can help them defend against predators or rivals.
