### The Walrus

The walrus, scientifically known as *Odobenus rosmarus*, is a massive marine mammal that thrives in the icy Arctic and sub-Arctic waters. Known for their long tusks and whiskered faces, walruses are social creatures that often gather in large groups on ice floes or beaches. They may look clumsy on land, but they’re surprisingly adept swimmers!
