## Meet the Fighters

### The Polar Bear

The polar bear, scientifically known as *Ursus maritimus*, is the giant of the Arctic. These incredible animals roam the icy seas and hunt primarily on sea ice, where they are perfectly adapted to their frigid environment. With their impressive size and strength, polar bears are often considered one of the most powerful carnivores on the planet.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 1,500 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 25 miles per hour
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 1,200 PSI

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,200 PSI
- Sharp claws up to 2 inches long
- Strong legs for powerful swimming

**Fighting Style:**
Polar bears are solitary hunters, using their strength and size to overpower seals and other prey. They often use stealth and ambush tactics, stalking their prey on the ice before launching a powerful attack. Their formidable size gives them the advantage in most confrontations, relying on brute force to secure their meals.

The cheetah, or *Acinonyx jubatus*, is the fastest land animal on Earth, famous for its incredible speed. With their unique adaptations, cheetahs are expert hunters, capable of taking down prey much larger than themselves.
