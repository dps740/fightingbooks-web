## Meet the Fighters

### The Jaguar

The jaguar, known scientifically as *Panthera onca*, prowls through the lush rainforests, swamps, and grasslands of Central and South America. This magnificent big cat is renowned for its strength, agility, and fierce hunting tactics. Often dubbed the third largest big cat in the world, jaguars are apex predators with exceptional skills that make them formidable foes.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 250 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long! That's as long as a school bus is tall!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 50 miles per hour! That's faster than a speeding car in a school zone!
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 1,500 PSI

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,500 PSI
- Sharp claws
- Stealthy approach

**Fighting Style:**
Jaguars are solitary hunters that rely on stealth to ambush their prey. With their incredible camouflage, they can sneak up on unsuspecting animals before delivering a powerful bite. They often strike from above or the shadows, using their speed and strength to overpower their target in a swift, decisive attack.

### The Ankylosaurus

Meet the ankylosaurus, scientifically known as *Ankylosaurus magniventris*. This fascinating dinosaur roamed the forests and plains during the Late Cretaceous period, approximately 66 million years ago. With its massive size and unique armor, the ankylosaurus was like a living tank, able to defend itself against any threat that came its way!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 6 tonnes! That's like 3 cars!
- **Length / Height:** About 26 feet long! Longer than a car but shorter than a school bus!
- **Top Speed:** Top speed of 8 mph! That's like a slow running kid!

**Weapons:**
- Clubbed tail (up to 3 feet long)
- Spiny back
- Thick armor

**Fighting Style:**
Ankylosaurus was a herbivore, but it was no pushover! Equipped with a clubbed tail and thick, armor-plated skin, this dinosaur could deliver a powerful blow to any attacker. Its fighting style was primarily defensive, using its tough exterior to withstand attacks while swinging its tail to fend off threats. If challenged, it could put up a fierce fight!
