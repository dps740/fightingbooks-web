## Meet the Fighters

### The Eagle

The Bald Eagle (Haliaeetus leucocephalus) is a powerful bird of prey known for its striking white head and tail, contrasting against its dark brown body. Found near large bodies of open water, they thrive where fish are plentiful and old-growth trees provide perfect nesting spots. As a symbol of strength and freedom, the Bald Eagle commands respect in its domain.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 3 to 6.3 kg (6.6 to 14 lbs)
- **Length / Height:** Wingspan of up to 7.5 feet! That's longer than a sedan (15 feet) laid on its side!
- **Top Speed:** Can dive at speeds of 30-40 mph! That's as fast as a car on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Sharp talons (up to 2.5 inches long)
- Strong beak
- Keen eyesight

**Fighting Style:**
Bald Eagles are solitary hunters that use their incredible eyesight to spot prey from great distances. They often employ an ambush tactic, diving down at high speeds to catch fish or small mammals off guard. Their powerful wings allow them to maneuver quickly and change direction mid-flight, making them formidable aerial attackers.

### The Chimera

The Chimera is a fearsome creature from ancient Greek mythology, described as having the head of a lion, a body of a goat, and a tail that ends with a snake. Believed to dwell in the rugged mountains of Lycia, this carnivorous beast is notorious for its chaotic and destructive nature. With its ability to breathe fire, the Chimera is as terrifying as it is awe-inspiring.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approximately 250 kg (550 lbs)
- **Length / Height:** About 10 feet long! That's like 2 school buses stacked end to end!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 30 miles per hour! That's as fast as a galloping horse!

**Weapons:**
- Fire-breathing (up to 1,000 degrees Fahrenheit)
- Sharp claws (about 1 foot long)
- Fangs (up to 6 inches long)

**Fighting Style:**
The Chimera is a fierce and aggressive fighter. It relies on its ability to breathe fire as a primary offensive weapon, while its sharp claws and fangs allow for close combat. Often attacking from a distance, it can create chaos before closing in on its prey. This creature does not shy away from a fight; it thrives in the chaos of battle.
