### The Stegosaurus

The stegosaurus is one of the most iconic dinosaurs, roaming the Earth during the late Jurassic period. This massive herbivore was found in dry, open woodlands and plains and is easily recognized by its distinctive double row of bony plates along its back and its spiked tail. While it may not have been the swiftest of creatures, the stegosaurus was a formidable presence on the prehistoric landscape.
