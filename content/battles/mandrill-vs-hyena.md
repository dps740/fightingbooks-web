### The Hyena

The spotted hyena (Crocuta crocuta) is a formidable predator found in various habitats across Africa, including savannas, grasslands, and woodlands. Often misunderstood, hyenas are highly social animals, living in clans that can consist of up to 80 members. They are notorious for their powerful jaws and scavenging abilities, capable of taking down large prey or stealing from other predators.
