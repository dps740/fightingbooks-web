They're fierce predators, capable of taking down large prey, including seals and even whales!

### The Wolf

The gray wolf, or *Canis lupus*, is a social animal that thrives in forests, tundras, and grasslands. Known for their pack mentality, wolves work together to hunt down larger prey, enabling them to dominate their territory. Their keen senses and agile bodies make them efficient hunters that can run great distances.
