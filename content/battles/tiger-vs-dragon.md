## Meet the Fighters

### The Tiger

The tiger, scientifically known as *Panthera tigris*, is the largest cat species in the world. Found in tropical forests, grasslands, and swamps across Asia, tigers are known for their striking orange fur with black stripes, which helps them camouflage in their natural habitat. They are solitary hunters, making them stealthy and efficient predators.

**Physical Stats:**

- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long including the tail! That's as long as a SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 30 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding CAR!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,000 PSI
- Sharp claws up to 3 inches long
- Strong legs for pouncing

**Fighting Style:**
Tigers are known for their ambush tactics. They often stalk their prey quietly before launching a powerful pounce. With their incredible strength and sharp claws, they can take down large animals. While they usually hunt alone, they rely on their stealth and power to surprise their opponents.

The dragon, or *Draco Ignis*, is a mythical beast that has captured the imagination of people for centuries. **Physical Stats:**

- **Length / Height:** 30 feet long (about the length of a school bus!)
