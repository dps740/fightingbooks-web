## Meet the Fighters

### The Tiger

The tiger, known scientifically as *Panthera tigris*, prowls the tropical forests, grasslands, and swamps of Asia. Renowned for its strength and striking beauty, this apex predator commands respect in the animal kingdom. With its iconic orange coat and distinctive black stripes, the tiger is not only a skilled hunter but also a symbol of power and grace.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 660 pounds!
- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long including the tail! That's as long as a SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 30 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding CAR!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,000 PSI
- Sharp claws up to 3 inches long
- Strong legs for pouncing

**Fighting Style:**
Tigers are solitary hunters, relying on stealth and strength. They typically stalk their prey, using their excellent camouflage to get within striking distance before launching a powerful pounce. Their impressive agility allows them to change direction quickly, making them formidable opponents in any confrontation.

### The King Cobra

The king cobra, or *Ophiophagus hannah*, slithers through the tropical forests and grasslands of Southeast Asia, where it reigns as the world's longest venomous snake. This snake is not only known for its impressive size but also for its potent venom, which can take down animals much larger than itself. With an intimidating hood and a striking body, the king cobra is a true marvel of nature.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 20 pounds, like a small dog!
- **Length / Height:** Up to 18 feet long! That's as long as a SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can slither at speeds of 3 miles per hour! That's as fast as a walking person!

**Weapons:**
- Venomous bite (up to 1.5 inches long)
- Muscular body
- Intimidating hood

**Fighting Style:**
The king cobra relies on its venomous bite as its main weapon. When threatened, it can stand tall and spread its hood to intimidate foes. If an opponent gets too close, a quick strike delivers a potent dose of venom, immobilizing its target. While it does not engage in physical combat like a tiger, its speed and stealth make it a lethal adversary.
