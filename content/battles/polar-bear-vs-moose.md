## Meet the Fighters

### The Polar Bear

The polar bear, known scientifically as *Ursus maritimus*, roams the icy landscapes of the Arctic. With its thick white fur and blubber, this carnivorous giant is expertly adapted to life on sea ice, where it primarily hunts seals. As the apex predator of its habitat, the polar bear is not just known for its size but also for its impressive hunting skills and strength.

- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 25 miles per hour

### The Moose

The moose, or *Alces alces*, is a magnificent herbivore found in forests and wetlands across North America, Europe, and Asia. Despite their gentle nature, they can be surprisingly aggressive, especially during mating season or when threatened.
