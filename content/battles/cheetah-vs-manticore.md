## Meet the Fighters

### The Cheetah

The cheetah, scientifically known as *Acinonyx jubatus*, roams the savannas and grasslands of Africa. Known for its remarkable speed and agility, this carnivorous feline is a master hunter, capable of accelerating faster than any other land animal. With its stunning coat and distinctive black tear markings, the cheetah is both beautiful and deadly.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 110 to 140 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder and 3.5 to 4.5 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 60 to 70 mph
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 7

**Weapons:**
- Sharp claws (up to 2 inches long)
- Powerful jaws
- Speedy legs

**Fighting Style:**
Cheetahs are solitary hunters that rely on stealth and speed to capture their prey. They often use camouflage to blend into their surroundings before launching a sudden sprint to ambush their target. Their impressive acceleration allows them to cover ground quickly, making them excellent at overtaking smaller animals like gazelles or impalas.
