With their golden manes and powerful physiques, lions are often referred to as the kings of the animal kingdom.

### The Brachiosaurus

The Brachiosaurus (Brachiosaurus altithorax) is one of the largest dinosaurs to ever roam the Earth, thriving in lush forests and floodplains millions of years ago. With its long neck and towering stature, this gentle giant could reach the treetops to munch on leaves and branches. While it’s often depicted in movies as a colossal creature, it was primarily a herbivore, peacefully grazing rather than engaging in combat.

**Physical Stats:**

**Fighting Style:**
