## Meet the Fighters

### The Tiger

The tiger (*Panthera tigris*) is a magnificent creature that roams the tropical forests, grasslands, and swamps of Asia. Renowned as one of the most powerful predators on the planet, this striking feline is known for its beautiful orange coat adorned with black stripes, and its solitary hunting style. Tigers are not just beautiful; they are fierce hunters that can take down prey much larger than themselves!

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**

While tigers typically hunt alone, their experience as solitary hunters makes them formidable opponents in any fight.

### The Velociraptor

The velociraptor (*Velociraptor mongoliensis*) was a small but fierce dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period. This agile predator thrived in dry, open areas like deserts and plains, using its speed and intelligence to hunt in packs. Often depicted as a cunning and agile hunter, the velociraptor has become a symbol of prehistoric ferocity!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** About 33 pounds! That's like a large dog!
- **Length / Height:** About 6.5 feet long! That's like a small car! (A sedan is ~15 feet)!
- **Top Speed:** Top speed of 24 mph! That's faster than a human sprinter!

**Weapons:**
- Clawed toes (up to 3 inches long)
- Sharp teeth
- Speed and agility

**Fighting Style:**
Velociraptors are known for their pack-hunting tactics, often working together to take down larger prey. They rely on their speed and agility to dodge attacks and strike quickly. With their large sickle-shaped claws, velociraptors can inflict serious damage in a fight, using swift movements and teamwork to outmaneuver their opponents.
