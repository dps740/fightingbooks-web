Meet the Great Horned Owl, or *Bubo virginianus*, one of the most widespread owls across North and South America. These adaptable birds can be found in forests, deserts, and even urban areas. With a wingspan that can reach up to 5 feet and powerful talons, they are fierce nocturnal hunters known for their impressive vertical takeoffs and silent flight.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 5.5 pounds!
- **Length / Height:** Body is 2 feet long with a wingspan up to 5 feet — wider than most adults are tall!

**Fighting Style:**
The Great Horned Owl utilizes its exceptional night vision and silent flight to surprise its prey. With impressive strength and agility, it can swoop down silently and deliver a powerful strike using its talons. This predator is not afraid to confront larger animals, showcasing its fearless nature, often attacking those much bigger than itself.
