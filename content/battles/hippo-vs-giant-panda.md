### The Giant Panda

The Giant Panda is a bear species native to the misty mountain bamboo forests of central China, particularly in the provinces of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu. These gentle giants are famous for their black and white fur and their diet that consists almost entirely of bamboo. Despite their cuddly appearance, giant pandas are powerful creatures that can defend themselves if threatened.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 330 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long

**Fighting Style:**
Giant pandas are not typically aggressive, but they can become formidable opponents if provoked. They rely on their sharp claws and strong bite to defend themselves against threats. Pandas are also excellent climbers and can escape danger quickly by climbing trees. When threatened, they may swipe with their strong forearms or use their powerful jaws to bite.
