### The Cheetah

The cheetah, or *Acinonyx jubatus*, is a sleek and agile feline that roams the savannas and grasslands of Africa. Their diet consists mainly of small to medium-sized animals like gazelles and impalas, which they must hunt daily to sustain their energy levels.
