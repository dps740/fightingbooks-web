## Meet the Fighters

### The Gorilla

Gorillas are the largest primates on Earth, found primarily in the tropical forests of Africa. Known for their immense strength and gentle nature, these intelligent creatures live in tight-knit family groups, often led by a dominant silverback male. Despite their size and power, gorillas are herbivores, munching on a variety of plants, leaves, and fruits.

### The Leopard

Leopards are agile and elusive feline hunters found in various habitats, including savannas, forests, and grasslands across Africa. Renowned for their striking spotted coats and incredible climbing skills, leopards are skilled predators that can take down prey much larger than themselves. They are solitary creatures, often stalking their prey with stealth and cunning.
