**Fighting Style:**

### The Cape Buffalo

The Cape Buffalo, or *Syncerus caffer*, can be found roaming the grasslands and savannas of Africa. Known for their massive size and intimidating presence, these creatures are often found in herds. Nicknamed "Black Death," they are revered for their strength and resilience, capable of defending themselves against even the fiercest predators.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 2,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 11.5 feet long! That's like 1/3 of a school bus! They stand about 5.5 feet tall at the shoulder.
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 35 mph! That's faster than a galloping horse!

**Weapons:**
- Horns up to 3.5 feet long!
- Powerful legs
- Thick skin

**Fighting Style:**
Cape Buffalos are known for their herd mentality. When threatened, they work together to fend off attackers, forming a protective circle around their young. Should an individual buffalo be targeted, they can charge with incredible speed, using their massive horns to deliver devastating blows to predators.
