## Meet the Fighters

### The Elephant

The African elephant, scientifically known as *Loxodonta africana*, is the largest land animal on Earth. These incredible creatures roam the savannas, forests, and grasslands of Africa, often forming tight-knit family groups. Known for their intelligence and social behavior, elephants are also equipped with impressive physical attributes that make them formidable opponents in the wild.

**Fighting Style:**
Elephants are powerful fighters that rely on their size and strength. They can charge at incredible speeds, using their tusks to ram opponents and their trunks to strike. ### The Hammerhead Shark

The hammerhead shark, with its distinctive hammer-shaped head, is a fascinating predator that roams the warm tropical and temperate oceans worldwide. Known for their keen hunting abilities and unique appearance, hammerheads thrive in diverse habitats, from coral reefs to deep open waters. They are social creatures, often traveling in schools, and are formidable hunters of various marine life.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 1,000 pounds!
- **Length / Height:** Up to 20 feet long — as long as a giraffe is tall!
- **Top Speed:** 25 mph in bursts — faster than most humans can run!

**Weapons:**
- Wide hammer head that pins prey to the ocean floor — like using a spatula!
- Rows of razor-sharp serrated teeth that constantly regrow
- Incredible speed bursts for ambush attacks

**Fighting Style:**
Hammerhead sharks are expert ambushers. They utilize their unique head shape to pin down prey like stingrays against the ocean floor. With rows of sharp teeth and incredible speed, they can launch surprise attacks on unsuspecting fish and other marine animals. Their 360-degree vision allows them to spot threats and prey from all angles.
