Known as one of the most formidable predators of its time, this dinosaur inhabited the lush forests and plains of North America. With its reputation as a terrifying carnivore, the T-Rex is often depicted as the ultimate dinosaur villain.

The Stegosaurus, scientifically known as Stegosaurus ungulatus, lived around 155 to 150 million years ago during the late Jurassic period. This herbivorous dinosaur was often found in dry, open woodlands and plains, munching on low-lying plants, leaves, and ferns. While it may look slow and clumsy, the Stegosaurus was well-equipped to defend itself.
