### The Walrus

The walrus, or *Odobenus rosmarus*, is an iconic marine mammal that thrives in the Arctic and sub-Arctic regions. With its distinctive tusks and massive body, the walrus is both a social creature and a formidable opponent. Known for their love of clams and mollusks, walruses can often be found lounging on ice floes or beaches in large groups.
