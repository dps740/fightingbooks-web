## Meet the Fighters

### The Lion

The lion, scientifically named *Panthera leo*, is an iconic big cat native to the grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands of Africa and India. Known for its regal mane and impressive social structure, lions live in groups called prides, where they work together to hunt and protect their territory. A lion's roar can be heard up to five miles away, asserting its dominance over the vast landscapes it calls home.

Though they often rely on ambush tactics, they can also confront threats directly, showcasing their strength and agility.

### The King Cobra

The king cobra, or *Ophiophagus hannah*, is the longest venomous snake in the world, thriving in tropical forests and grasslands across Southeast Asia. This snake is known for its impressive length—up to 18 feet! With a reputation for its powerful venom and impressive defensive displays, the king cobra is a master of camouflage, blending seamlessly into its environment.
