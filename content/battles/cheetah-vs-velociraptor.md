## Meet the Fighters

### The Cheetah

The cheetah, scientifically known as *Acinonyx jubatus*, is the fastest terrestrial animal on the planet, roaming the vast savannas and grasslands of Africa. Cheetahs are known for their short bursts of incredible acceleration, which they use to chase down prey like gazelles and impalas.

### The Velociraptor

The Velociraptor, or *Velociraptor mongoliensis*, was a small but formidable dinosaur that roamed the dry, open areas of what is now Mongolia and China. They are often depicted as cunning pack hunters, utilizing their intelligence and agility to outsmart their prey. Although small, Velociraptors were fierce, hunting in groups to take down larger animals.

**Fighting Style:**
Velociraptors are known for their pack-hunting tactics, which involve highly coordinated attacks on their prey. With quick reflexes and the ability to change direction rapidly, they can dodge attacks and strike from unexpected angles. Their sickle-shaped claws are particularly deadly, allowing them to slash and cause serious damage.
