### The Polar Bear

The polar bear, scientifically known as *Ursus maritimus*, is a magnificent creature that roams the icy Arctic regions, primarily on sea ice. As the largest land carnivore, they are well-adapted to their frigid habitat, and they are known for their incredible swimming abilities and keen sense of smell.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 1,500 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Up to 25 miles per hour
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 1,200 PSI

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,200 PSI
- Sharp claws up to 2 inches long
- Strong legs for powerful swimming

**Fighting Style:**
Polar bears are solitary hunters that rely on stealth and brute strength to catch seals and other prey. They use their exceptional swimming skills to travel between ice flows and can deliver devastating blows with their paws to incapacitate their targets.
