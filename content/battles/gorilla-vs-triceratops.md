## Meet the Fighters

### The Gorilla

The gorilla is a remarkable creature that inhabits the lush tropical forests of Africa. With a thick, muscular build and an intelligent gaze, these primates are often viewed as gentle giants—unless provoked! Gorillas are social animals, living in family groups and showcasing complex behaviors that demonstrate their remarkable cognitive abilities.

- **Weight:** Up to 400 pounds! 
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet tall and 12 feet long when standing on all fours! That's as tall as a door!

**Weapons:**

- Powerful jaws
- Intimidating chest thump

**Fighting Style:**
Gorillas often rely on their sheer strength and size in confrontations. They can be intimidating without even engaging in a fight, using powerful chest thumps to ward off threats. If necessary, they can charge at an opponent, using their strong arms and jaws to deliver powerful blows. While they typically resolve conflicts through displays and vocalizations, they can fight fiercely when protecting their group.

### The Triceratops

The Triceratops is one of the most recognizable dinosaurs, roaming the open plains and forests of North America about 68 million years ago. With its three formidable horns and a large bony frill, this herbivore was not only an impressive sight but also a vital part of its ecosystem. Triceratops are believed to have been social creatures, often found in herds, and used their impressive weapons for both defense and social displays.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** About 12 tons! 
- **Length / Height:** 30 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Top speed of 20 mph! That's faster than the fastest human and scary when 12,000 pounds is charging at you!

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
Triceratops were not shy when it came to defending themselves. They would use their massive horns for thrusting and goring, while their sturdy bodies allowed them to charge at threats. When faced with danger, they could quickly turn to confront an attacker, making use of their strong legs and impressive horns to deliver powerful strikes.
