## Meet the Fighters

The gray wolf (Canis lupus) is a highly social carnivore that thrives in various habitats, including forests, tundras, and grasslands. Known for its keen hunting skills and strong pack mentality, the wolf is a skilled predator, often hunting larger animals like deer and elk. With a reputation for intelligence and teamwork, wolves are both feared and respected in the animal kingdom.

**Fighting Style:**
Wolves typically hunt in packs, using teamwork to bring down prey. They often work together to surround their target, utilizing their speed and strength to overpower it. Wolves can also fight solo when necessary, relying on their agility and sharp teeth to defend themselves against threats.

### The Pteranodon

The Pteranodon (Pteranodon longiceps) is a remarkable flying reptile that lived during the Late Cretaceous period, about 70 million years ago. With a massive wingspan of up to 33 feet, Pteranodon is one of the largest known flying creatures. Although not a dinosaur, it was an impressive predator that thrived in coastal areas, where it hunted for fish and squid.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approx. 50-100 pounds (approx.)

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
The Pteranodon primarily relies on its flight for defense and offense. With its impressive wingspan, it can soar high above the ground, using its keen eyesight to spot potential threats or prey. Though it may not engage in direct combat often, its beak and claws can deliver powerful strikes if necessary.
