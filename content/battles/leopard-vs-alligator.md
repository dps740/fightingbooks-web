### The Alligator

The American alligator, or *Alligator mississippiensis*, is a true heavyweight of the wetlands, found in swamps, rivers, and lakes throughout the southeastern USA. With its tough, armored skin and powerful bite, this reptile is a master ambush predator.

**Fighting Style:**
