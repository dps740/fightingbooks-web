## Meet the Fighters

### The Tiger

The tiger, scientifically known as *Panthera tigris*, is a magnificent carnivorous feline found primarily in tropical forests, grasslands, and swamps across Asia. Known for their striking orange coat with black stripes, tigers are stealthy hunters that command respect in the animal kingdom.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 220 to 660 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long including the tail! That's as long as a school bus!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 30 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding car!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,000 PSI
- Sharp claws up to 3 inches long
- Strong legs for pouncing

**Fighting Style:**
Tigers are solitary hunters, relying on their incredible stealth to stalk and ambush their prey. They often use their powerful legs and sharp claws to leap onto their target, delivering a fatal bite. Their ability to camouflage in dense vegetation allows them to sneak up on animals without being detected.

### The Great White Shark

The great white shark, scientifically known as *Carcharodon carcharias*, is one of the most formidable predators in the ocean. Found in coastal waters worldwide, these sharks are notorious for their size and hunting prowess, often targeting seals and other marine creatures.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Over 2,400 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 20 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can swim up to 25 miles per hour! That's faster than a speeding car!

**Weapons:**
- Jaws with 300 sharp teeth, each up to 3 inches long!
- Powerful tail
- Speedy body

**Fighting Style:**
Great white sharks utilize a strategy of ambush and speed. They often circle their prey before launching a surprise attack from below, using their powerful jaws to deliver a devastating bite. They are also capable of breaching the water's surface, leaping out in pursuit of seals, which showcases their strength and agility.
