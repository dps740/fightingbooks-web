### The Crocodile

The saltwater crocodile, or *Crocodylus porosus*, is renowned as the largest living reptile on Earth. Found in coastal areas, rivers, and swamps, these formidable predators have existed for millions of years. With their armored skin and powerful jaws, crocodiles are masters of ambush, lying in wait for unsuspecting prey.
