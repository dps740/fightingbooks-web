## Meet the Fighters

### The Lion

The lion, scientifically known as *Panthera leo*, is a majestic creature that roams the grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands of Africa and India. Known as the "king of the jungle," this iconic big cat is famous for its powerful roar and social structure, living in groups called prides.

- **Length / Height:** About 8 to 10 feet long from nose to tail
- **Top Speed:** Up to 50 miles per hour for short bursts

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**

When fighting, a lion can use its speed and strength to pounce on its opponent, delivering a fatal bite or claw strike.

### The Orca

The orca, also known as the killer whale and scientifically classified as *Orcinus orca*, is an awe-inspiring marine mammal found in oceans worldwide, especially in colder waters. While they are called "whales," orcas are actually the largest members of the dolphin family, known for their intelligence and complex social structures.
