Elephants are herbivores, relying on a diet of grass, leaves, fruits, and bark, and can consume an astonishing 300 pounds of food a day!

**Fighting Style:**

### The Ankylosaurus

The Ankylosaurus, scientifically named *Ankylosaurus magniventris*, thrived in the forests and plains of the Late Cretaceous period. This dinosaur is famous for its impressive armor, which made it one of the toughest herbivores of its time. With a length of about 26 feet, the Ankylosaurus would have been a formidable sight during its reign, munching on low-growing plants and ferns.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 6 tons (approximately 12,000 pounds)
- **Length / Height:** About 26 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** A top speed of 8 mph! That's like a slow running kid!

**Weapons:**
- Clubbed tail (up to 3 feet long)
- Spiny back
- Thick armor

**Fighting Style:**
Ankylosaurus was built for defense, relying on its tough armor to withstand attacks. When threatened, this dinosaur would swing its powerful clubbed tail to strike at predators, potentially causing serious damage. Its low center of gravity allowed it to remain stable even when swinging its tail, making it a fierce opponent.
