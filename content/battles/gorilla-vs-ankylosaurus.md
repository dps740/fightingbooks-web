**Fighting Style:**

### The Ankylosaurus

The Ankylosaurus, scientifically known as *Ankylosaurus magniventris*, roamed the forests and plains during the Late Cretaceous period. This herbivorous dinosaur is famous for its unique body armor and formidable defensive capabilities. With its ability to munch on low-growing plants and ferns, the Ankylosaurus was well-equipped for survival in its time.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 6 tonnes (about 13,227 lbs)
- **Length / Height:** About 26 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Top speed of 8 mph! That's like a slow running kid!

**Weapons:**
- Clubbed tail (up to 3 feet long)
- Spiny back
- Thick armor

**Fighting Style:**
Ankylosaurus relies heavily on its thick armor for defense. When threatened, it can swing its clubbed tail with significant force, delivering powerful blows to deter attackers. This dinosaur's low center of gravity prevents it from being easily toppled, allowing it to stay grounded during a fight.
