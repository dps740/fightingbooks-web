## Meet the Fighters

### The Wolf

The Gray Wolf (Canis lupus) is a highly social creature known for its pack behavior. These highly intelligent animals thrive in forests, tundras, and grasslands, often living in family groups that work together to hunt and protect their territory. Renowned for their strong howls that can communicate over distances of up to 6 miles, wolves are not just skilled hunters; they are also incredibly adaptable.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 110 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.5 feet long (including tail) - ABOUT AS LONG AS A SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 40 mph - FASTER THAN A SPRINTING HUMAN!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 1,500 PSI)
- Sharp claws
- Keen sense of smell

**Fighting Style:**
Wolves are known for their pack mentality, often hunting in groups to take down larger prey. When fighting, they use their powerful jaws and sharp teeth to deliver strong bites, relying on teamwork to outmaneuver and overpower opponents. When alone, they may adopt a more cautious approach, utilizing their speed and agility to evade attackers.

### The Leopard

The Leopard (Panthera pardus) is a solitary and stealthy hunter, perfectly adapted to life in the savannas, forests, and grasslands of Africa and parts of Asia. With their beautiful spotted fur providing excellent camouflage, leopards are skilled ambush predators, often stalking their prey before launching a surprise attack. Known for their agility and strength, they are equally comfortable climbing trees or swimming in rivers.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Between 66 to 176 pounds
- **Length / Height:** LEOPARDS CAN BE UP TO 6.25 FEET LONG! THAT'S LIKE A SMALL SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 36 mph! That's faster than a galloping horse but slower than their cousin the cheetah (70 mph)!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 2 inches long)
- Sharp claws
- Agile body

**Fighting Style:**
Leopards are masters of stealth and surprise, often utilizing their climbing ability to ambush prey from above. In a fight, they rely on their strong muscles and sharp claws to strike quickly and retreat if necessary. Their agility allows them to dodge incoming attacks, making them formidable opponents.
