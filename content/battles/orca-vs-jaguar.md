## Meet the Fighters

### The Orca

The orca, or "killer whale," is one of the ocean's most formidable predators. Found in oceans worldwide, especially in colder waters, these intelligent creatures travel in groups known as pods and are known for their complex social structures. With their striking black and white coloration, orcas are not just beautiful; they are also incredibly efficient hunters.

### The Jaguar

The jaguar is a majestic big cat known for its strength and agility. Found in rainforests, swamps, and grasslands throughout Central and South America, these solitary hunters are stealthy and powerful. Jaguars are often considered the third largest big cat in the world, and their distinctive rosettes help them blend into their environment.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 250 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 50 mph
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 1,500 PSI

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,500 PSI
- Sharp claws
- Stealthy approach

**Fighting Style:**
Jaguars are known for their ambush tactics. They sneak up on their prey, using their excellent camouflage to remain undetected until the last moment. Once they strike, they use their powerful jaws to deliver a fatal bite that can penetrate the shells of armored reptiles like turtles and caimans.
