With a sleek body designed for speed, they are the ultimate hunters of the sea, often seen patrolling the waters for seals and other prey.

- **Length / Height:** Up to 20 feet long!

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
Great White Sharks are known for their ambush tactics. They often sneak up on prey from below, utilizing their speed and powerful jaws to deliver a devastating bite. They typically hunt alone, relying on their excellent sense of smell and ability to detect vibrations in the water to locate food.

### The Anaconda

The Green Anaconda, scientifically known as *Eunectes murinus*, is the largest snake in the world, capable of growing up to 30 feet long! Found in swamps and rivers throughout South America, these massive snakes are famous for their incredible strength and stealth. They are excellent swimmers and can remain submerged for long periods, making them experts in surprise attacks.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Over 500 lbs
- **Length / Height:** Up to 30 feet long! That's longer than a SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can swim at 1.5 mph, like a slow jog!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful muscles (30 feet long)
- Camouflage
- Strong jaws

**Fighting Style:**
Anacondas rely on their immense strength and stealth to capture prey. They often lie in wait, camouflaged in the water, before striking with lightning speed. Once they have a grip on their victim, they coil around it and constrict until the prey is subdued, using their powerful muscles to finish the job.
