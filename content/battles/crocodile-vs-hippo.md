## Meet the Fighters

### The Crocodile

The Saltwater Crocodile, or *Crocodylus porosus*, is the largest reptile on Earth and a master of ambush tactics. Typically found in coastal areas, rivers, and swamps, these formidable predators are known for their stealth and ferocity. With a reputation for being fierce and cunning, they are often the apex predators in their ecosystems.

- **Weight:** Up to 2,200 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 23 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can swim up to 20 mph! That's as fast as a running dog!
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 3,700 pounds!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 3,700 pounds!
- Sharp teeth
- Long tail

**Fighting Style:**
Crocodiles are ambush hunters, often lying in wait for their prey to come close before launching a surprise attack. They rely on their powerful jaws and stealth to overpower their enemies. While they typically hunt solo, they can work together when needed, especially during mating seasons or when defending territory.

### The Hippo

The Common Hippopotamus, or *Hippopotamus amphibius*, is one of the largest land mammals and is known for its massive size and surprisingly aggressive nature. Found in rivers and lakes across Africa, hippos spend most of their time submerged in water, keeping cool while they graze on grass. They are often underestimated due to their seemingly docile appearance, but they are incredibly dangerous.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 4,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 16.5 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 19 mph! That's faster than most kids can sprint!
- **Bite Force / Strength:** Not specified, but their jaws can open 150 degrees wide!

**Weapons:**
- Massive jaws with 20-inch teeth
- Thick skin
- Powerful legs

**Fighting Style:**
Hippos are known for their aggressive and territorial behavior, especially when it comes to protecting their young. They use their enormous size and powerful jaws to intimidate and attack threats. Hippos can charge quickly on land and are surprisingly agile despite their bulk, making them formidable opponents in any confrontation.
