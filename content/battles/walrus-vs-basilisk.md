## Meet the Fighters

### The Walrus

The walrus, scientifically known as *Odobenus rosmarus*, is a massive marine mammal found in the icy waters of the Arctic and sub-Arctic regions. Known for its impressive tusks and blubbery bodies, walruses are social creatures that often gather in large groups, creating a sight to behold. They are primarily carnivores, feasting on clams, mollusks, and various sea creatures.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 2,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 12 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can swim up to 35 mph! That's like a speeding car on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Tusks up to 3 feet long!
- Strong flippers
- Massive body weight

**Fighting Style:**
Walruses are not known for their agility on land, but in the water, they can be surprisingly quick and powerful. They often rely on their size and tusks during confrontations, using their formidable weight to intimidate or overpower opponents. While walruses are generally peaceful, they can become aggressive when threatened, using a combination of tusk jabs and their bulk to defend themselves.

### The Basilisk

The basilisk is a mythical creature often depicted as a serpent or lizard, with origins in European folklore. It is said to dwell in dark forests and caves, lurking in the shadows and striking fear into the hearts of those who dare to approach. Known as the "King of Serpents," the basilisk possesses a deadly gaze capable of turning living beings to stone, making it one of the most formidable foes in mythology.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** (approx.) 300 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long! That's like 2 school buses lined up!
- **Top Speed:** Can sprint up to 30 mph! That's as fast as a car driving through a neighborhood!

**Weapons:**
- Deadly gaze (can turn creatures to stone)
- Sharp claws (up to 6 inches long)
- Venomous bite

**Fighting Style:**
Basilisks are known for their cunning and stealth. They typically rely on their deadly gaze to incapacitate opponents from a distance, avoiding direct confrontation whenever possible. With their speed and agility, they can swiftly escape if the situation turns unfavorable. When forced to engage, their sharp claws and venomous bite come into play, making them a serious threat in close combat.
