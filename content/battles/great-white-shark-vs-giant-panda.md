### The Great White Shark

With a reputation for being one of the ocean's most efficient hunters, the Great White strikes fear into the hearts of its prey.

**Weapons:**

### The Giant Panda

The Giant Panda, scientifically known as Ailuropoda melanoleuca, is a gentle giant found in the misty mountain bamboo forests of central China. While they might look cuddly and harmless, these bears are surprisingly strong and can be quite fierce when threatened. With their unique diet, primarily consisting of bamboo, Giant Pandas have adapted to thrive in their mountainous habitat.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 330 lbs
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 mph in short bursts!
- **Bite Force / Strength:** Bite force of 292 PSI

**Weapons:**
- Jaw muscles and teeth strong enough to crush thick bamboo stalks
- Sharp claws on powerful forearms for climbing and swiping
- A special sixth finger (pseudo-thumb) for incredible grip strength

**Fighting Style:**
While Giant Pandas are not aggressive by nature, they can defend themselves if necessary. They use their powerful claws to swipe at threats and can bite with a strong jaw. Pandas are also excellent climbers, allowing them to escape danger quickly by climbing trees.
