## Meet the Fighters

### The Rhino

The White Rhino, or *Ceratotherium simum*, roams the grasslands and savannas of Africa. Known for its massive size and impressive horn, this herbivore often stands its ground against threats, earning a reputation as a fierce defender of its territory.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 5,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 13 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 30 mph

**Weapons:**
- **HORN:** Up to 3 feet long!
- **THICK SKIN**
- **POWERFUL LEGS**

**Fighting Style:**
Rhinos are solitary fighters, relying on their brute strength and size to intimidate foes. They charge at opponents when threatened, using their massive horns to deliver devastating blows. While they may not be the fastest, their aggressive nature and sheer power make them formidable opponents.

### The Dragon

The Fire-Breathing Dragon, scientifically known as *Draco Ignis*, is a mythical creature that dwells in mountains and caves. With its ability to soar through the skies and unleash fire, this carnivorous beast strikes fear into the hearts of those who dare to cross its path.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 1,500 pounds
- **Length / Height:** 30 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 60 mph

**Weapons:**
- **Fire Breath (up to 1,000°F)**
- **Sharp Claws (2 feet long)**
- **Tail Swipe (5 feet wide)**

**Fighting Style:**
Dragons are aerial fighters, using their incredible speed and agility to outmaneuver opponents. They can attack from above, breathing fire to scorch their foes while remaining out of reach. Their sharp claws and powerful tail add to their arsenal, making them versatile hunters.
