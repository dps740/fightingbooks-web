## Meet the Fighters

### The Hippo

The common hippopotamus, or hippo for short, is a hefty herbivore that can be found lounging in rivers and lakes across Africa. With their large bodies and surprisingly agile movements, hippos are both majestic and formidable. They spend much of their day submerged in water to keep cool, but don’t let their lazy appearance fool you—they are known for their fierce tempers!

### The Walrus

The walrus is a massive marine mammal that thrives in the Arctic and sub-Arctic regions. With their long tusks and playful personalities, they are often seen lounging on ice floes or diving for delicious clams and mollusks. Although walruses are social creatures, they can be quite protective of their territory and young, making them fierce opponents when necessary.
