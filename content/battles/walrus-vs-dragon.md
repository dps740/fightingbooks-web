## Meet the Fighters

### The Walrus

The walrus, known scientifically as Odobenus rosmarus, is an impressive marine mammal found in the cold Arctic and sub-Arctic regions. Renowned for their large size and distinctive tusks, walruses are social creatures often seen lounging together on ice floes. Their impressive blubber layer keeps them warm in icy waters, and they can dive deep to hunt for their favorite snacks—clams and mollusks!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 2,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 12 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can swim up to 35 mph! That's like a speeding car on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Tusks up to 3 feet long!
- Strong flippers
- Massive body weight

**Fighting Style:**
Walruses mainly use their size and strength when it comes to fighting. With their powerful tusks, they can deliver serious blows to threats. They usually fight solo and can be surprisingly agile in the water, using their flippers to maneuver quickly. When threatened, they may also dive deep to escape danger.

### The Dragon

The fire-breathing dragon, scientifically known as Draco Ignis, is a legendary creature found in the lofty mountains and dark caves of folklore. These majestic beasts are known for their immense size and ability to breathe fire at temperatures reaching up to 1,000°F! Dragons are often depicted as fierce predators, hunting large animals like deer and, in some tales, even other dragons.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 1,500 pounds
- **Length / Height:** 30 feet long (about the length of a school bus!)
- **Top Speed:** 60 mph! That's as fast as a car on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Fire Breath (up to 1,000°F)
- Sharp Claws (2 feet long)
- Tail Swipe (5 feet wide)

**Fighting Style:**
Dragons are known for their aerial prowess and fierce combat techniques. They attack from above, using their fire breath to scorch enemies from a distance. Their sharp claws and powerful tails allow them to engage in close-quarter combat, making them versatile fighters. With their ability to fly, dragons can also dodge incoming attacks effectively.
