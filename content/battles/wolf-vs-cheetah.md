## Meet the Fighters

### The Wolf

The gray wolf (Canis lupus) is a social and intelligent predator known for its strong pack mentality. Wolves typically inhabit forests, tundras, and grasslands, where they roam vast territories in search of food. With a reputation as skilled hunters, these animals are best known for their teamwork when they hunt larger mammals like deer and elk.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 50-110 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.5 feet long (including tail) - ABOUT AS LONG AS A SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 40 mph - FASTER THAN A SPRINTING HUMAN!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 1,500 PSI)
- Sharp claws
- Keen sense of smell

**Fighting Style:**
Wolves are pack hunters, often using teamwork to corner and take down their prey. They rely on their strong jaws and sharp teeth to deliver deadly bites. When fighting, they may use ambush tactics or engage in direct confrontation, using their size and strength to outmaneuver opponents.

### The Cheetah

The cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus) is the fastest land animal on Earth, renowned for its incredible speed and agility. Native to the savannas and grasslands of Africa, cheetahs are built for speed, with long legs and a lightweight body. These agile felines primarily hunt small to medium-sized animals like gazelles and impalas, often relying on their speed to make quick kills.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 110-140 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder and 3.5 to 4.5 feet long. That's about the length of a small sedan!
- **Top Speed:** Top speed of 60 to 70 mph! That's faster than a school bus on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Sharp claws (up to 2 inches long)
- Powerful jaws
- Speedy legs

**Fighting Style:**
Cheetahs are known for their explosive bursts of speed, allowing them to chase down and capture prey in a matter of seconds. They rely on stealth and quick acceleration, often closing in on their targets before launching a high-speed attack. During a fight, they might use their powerful jaws and sharp claws to defend themselves or capture prey.
