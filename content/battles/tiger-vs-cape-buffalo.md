### The Cape Buffalo

The Cape buffalo, or *Syncerus caffer*, is a massive herbivore found in the grasslands and savannas of Africa. Known for their strength and unpredictable nature, these animals travel in herds, providing safety in numbers. They are often regarded as one of Africa's most dangerous animals.

- Thick skin

**Fighting Style:**
Cape buffalo are known for their brutal charging attacks, especially when threatened. They defend themselves fiercely, using their massive horns as weapons. Buffalo often fight in groups, working together to fend off predators and protect their young.
