### The Brachiosaurus

Brachiosaurus altithorax, the giant of the Jurassic period, roamed the Earth about 154-150 million years ago. This herbivorous dinosaur was known for its long neck and enormous size, reaching heights of up to 40 feet. Brachiosaurus lived in forests and floodplains, munching on leaves from the tallest trees.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** About 62 tons
- **Length / Height:** 85 feet long and 40 feet tall!
- **Top Speed:** Top speed of 10 miles per hour! That's like running as fast as a person on a bike!
