## Meet the Fighters

- **Bite Force / Strength:** 7

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
The Green Python relies on its incredible stealth and striking abilities. It typically ambushes its prey, using its powerful muscles to constrict and suffocate. This solitary hunter often uses camouflage to blend into its environment, making it nearly invisible until it’s too late for its target!

### The Cerberus

Cerberus is a mythological creature that guards the gates of Hades, preventing the dead from escaping the Underworld. This fearsome beast is depicted as having three ferocious heads, each capable of biting simultaneously. With a thick coat of fur and a reputation for scaring off even the bravest heroes, Cerberus is a legendary symbol of fear.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approx. 800 pounds
- **Length / Height:** About 30 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 25 miles per hour!
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 7

**Weapons:**
- Three fierce heads, each about 3 feet wide
- Sharp claws
- Poisonous breath

**Fighting Style:**
Cerberus employs an aggressive fighting style, using its three heads to attack from different angles simultaneously. This multi-headed approach allows it to intimidate foes and overwhelm them with sheer ferocity. Additionally, its powerful legs enable it to charge at incredible speeds, making it a formidable opponent.
