### The Velociraptor

The Velociraptor, scientifically known as *Velociraptor mongoliensis*, was a small but fierce predator that thrived in the dry, open areas like deserts and plains during the late Cretaceous period. Often depicted in movies as a larger creature, these dinosaurs were actually about the size of a turkey, making them fast, agile hunters.

**Physical Stats:**

- Clawed toes (up to 3 inches long)

**Fighting Style:**
Velociraptors were pack hunters, using their speed and agility to overwhelm their prey. They relied on quick strikes, using their sharp claws to inflict damage and their intelligence to coordinate attacks with others in their pack. Their quick reflexes help them dodge counterattacks, allowing them to strike and retreat effectively.
