## Meet the Fighters

The moose, scientifically known as *Alces alces*, roams the forests and wetlands of North America, Europe, and parts of Asia. They love to munch on leaves, twigs, and aquatic plants, making them true giants of the herbivore world!

### The Python

The green python, scientifically known as *Morelia viridis*, slithers through the tropical rainforests of New Guinea and Australia. These strikingly beautiful snakes are known for their vibrant green scales, allowing them to blend seamlessly into the foliage. As carnivores, they prey on small mammals and birds, using their powerful muscles to constrict and suffocate their prey.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 20 pounds (approx.)

**Fighting Style:**
Pythons are ambush predators, using their camouflage to hide in trees and wait for unsuspecting prey. When they strike, they do so swiftly, wrapping their bodies around their target to constrict and overpower it. While they are not aggressive fighters, their stealth and strength make them lethal when the opportunity arises.
