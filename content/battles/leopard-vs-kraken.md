## Meet the Fighters

### The Leopard

The leopard, scientifically known as *Panthera pardus*, roams the savannas, forests, and grasslands of Africa and parts of Asia. This powerful predator is known for its stunning spotted coat and incredible agility, making it one of nature's most skilled hunters.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 176 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.25 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 36 mph

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 2 inches long)
- Sharp claws
- Agile body

**Fighting Style:**
Leopards are solitary hunters that prefer to ambush their prey. With their amazing camouflage, they can stealthily stalk their victims before launching a surprise attack. They utilize their strength and agility to overpower animals much larger than themselves.

### The Kraken

The kraken is a mythological creature said to inhabit the deep ocean waters, often depicted as a giant squid or octopus. Legends describe it as a monstrous beast capable of dragging entire ships under the waves, making it one of the most feared creatures of the sea.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** As much as 10 elephants (approx. 50,000 pounds)
- **Length / Height:** Up to 100 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can swim at speeds of 25 mph

**Weapons:**
- Tentacles up to 30 feet long
- Beak like a giant parrot
- Ink cloud for escape

**Fighting Style:**
The kraken relies on its massive size and long tentacles to ensnare its enemies. It can create whirlpools to confuse and trap prey, and its beak delivers a powerful bite. When threatened, it can escape by releasing a cloud of ink, making it a slippery opponent.
