### The Kraken

The Kraken is a mythical creature that has captivated imaginations for centuries. Its beak can deliver a deadly bite, while its ability to create ink clouds allows it to escape if the battle turns against it. Often depicted as a solitary creature, the Kraken relies on its massive size and strength to dominate its foes.
