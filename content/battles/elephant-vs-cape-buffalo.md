Often seen in herds, elephants are revered for their nurturing behavior and strong family bonds.

### The Cape Buffalo

The Cape Buffalo, also known as *Syncerus caffer*, is a formidable opponent found in the grasslands and savannas of Africa. Cape Buffalos are social creatures, typically living in herds where they display strong bonds and cooperative defense strategies.

Buffalos typically work in groups, relying on herd tactics to fend off predators or threats.
