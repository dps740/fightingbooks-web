## Meet the Fighters

### The Polar Bear

The polar bear, known scientifically as *Ursus maritimus*, is a powerful carnivore that roams the frozen Arctic regions, primarily on sea ice. It is the largest land carnivore, with a reputation for being an incredible hunter, primarily preying on seals. With its thick layer of blubber and striking white fur, the polar bear is perfectly adapted to life in the frigid north, making it a formidable opponent in any fight.

### The Jaguar

The jaguar, scientifically known as *Panthera onca*, is a sleek and powerful big cat native to the rainforests, swamps, and grasslands of Central and South America. Renowned for its strength and agility, the jaguar is the third-largest big cat in the world. With its distinctive rosettes and exceptional climbing skills, this agile predator is a master of stealth, making it a formidable hunter.

**Physical Stats:**

In a fight, a jaguar would likely rely on its speed and agility, striking quickly and then retreating to avoid direct confrontation.
