## Meet the Fighters

### The Leopard

Leopards, known scientifically as *Panthera pardus*, are magnificent big cats that roam the savannas, forests, and grasslands of Africa and parts of Asia. Renowned for their incredible strength and adaptability, leopards are skilled hunters that can take down prey much larger than themselves.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 176 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.25 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 36 mph

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 2 inches long)
- Sharp claws
- Agile body

**Fighting Style:**
Leopards are solitary hunters that often rely on stealth and ambush tactics. They use their spotted fur to blend into their surroundings, stalking their prey before launching a sudden attack. With a combination of speed and strength, they can tackle animals as large as deer and antelope.

### The Chimera

Chimeras are mythical creatures from ancient Greek lore, often depicted as having the head of a lion, the body of a goat, and a snake for a tail. They inhabit the rugged mountains of Lycia and are known for their chaotic and fearsome nature. This creature is not just a figment of imagination but a symbol of power and danger!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approximately 600 pounds
- **Length / Height:** About 10 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 30 mph

**Weapons:**
- Fire-breathing (up to 1,000 degrees Fahrenheit)
- Sharp claws (about 1 foot long)
- Fangs (up to 6 inches long)

**Fighting Style:**
Chimeras are known for their ferocity and ability to breathe fire, making them a formidable opponent. They can attack from a distance with their fiery breath while using their sharp claws and fangs in close combat. With their intimidating roar, they can scare off potential threats before engaging in battle.
