The Triceratops, scientifically known as *Triceratops horridus*, roamed the open plains and forests of North America over 66 million years ago. This herbivorous dinosaur is one of the most recognizable creatures of the late Cretaceous period, thanks to its massive size and three distinctive horns. With its bony frill and powerful build, the Triceratops was a formidable creature, often relying on its impressive defenses to fend off predators.

- **Top Speed:** 20 mph

**Weapons:**

Cerberus, the fearsome guardian of the Underworld, is a mythological creature with three heads, each capable of delivering a powerful bite. This carnivorous beast is famous for terrifying anyone who dared to escape the Underworld, making it a legendary figure in tales of bravery and heroism.
