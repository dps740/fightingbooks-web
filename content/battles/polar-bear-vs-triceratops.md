The polar bear, known scientifically as *Ursus maritimus*, is the largest land carnivore and a true icon of the Arctic. Polar bears are powerful swimmers, often seen gliding through icy waters, and they have a reputation for being fierce predators, capable of taking down large prey.

With its three impressive horns and large bony frill, the triceratops was well-equipped for defense against predators, and it was known for its formidable size and strength.
