- **Length / Height:** Up to 30 feet long! That's longer than a SCHOOL BUS!

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
The Anaconda primarily employs a stealthy ambush tactic, using its excellent camouflage to blend into its surroundings. This snake often hunts solo, relying on its sheer strength and cunning to overpower its prey.

### The Triceratops

Meet the Triceratops, scientifically known as *Triceratops horridus*, a giant herbivorous dinosaur that roamed the open plains and forests of North America over 66 million years ago. With its three impressive horns and large bony frill, the Triceratops is known for its formidable defense against predators.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** About 12 tons! That's like 3 elephants!
- **Length / Height:** 30 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can reach speeds of 20 mph! That's faster than the fastest human!

**Weapons:**

- Beak for biting
- Heavy body for charging

**Fighting Style:**
The Triceratops is a defensive powerhouse. When threatened, it can charge at high speeds, using its impressive horns to fend off predators. This dinosaur often relies on its massive size and strength to intimidate attackers, and it’s not afraid to put on a show of force when it needs to protect itself or its herd.
