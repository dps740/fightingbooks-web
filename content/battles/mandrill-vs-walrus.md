Their vibrant facial features aren’t just for show; they play a vital role in communication and social interaction within their troops.

### The Walrus

Meet the walrus (Odobenus rosmarus), the gentle giant of the Arctic and sub-Arctic regions. These massive marine mammals can grow to an impressive length of up to 12 feet and can weigh as much as a small car—over 2,000 pounds! Walruses are primarily carnivorous, feasting on clams, mollusks, and other sea creatures. With their characteristic tusks and whiskered faces, walruses are easily recognizable and often seen lounging on ice floes in large social groups.
