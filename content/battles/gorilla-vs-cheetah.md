## Meet the Fighters

### The Gorilla

The gorilla, known scientifically as *Gorilla gorilla*, is a giant primate that roams the lush tropical forests of Africa. These gentle giants are herbivorous, munching on a wide variety of plants, including leaves, stems, and fruits. Despite their massive size and intimidating appearance, gorillas are known for their complex social structures and intelligence, often communicating through a range of vocalizations.

**Fighting Style:**
Gorillas are primarily peaceful creatures but can become fierce when threatened. They rely on their immense strength, often using powerful blows from their arms to defend themselves. Their fighting style includes intimidating displays, such as chest thumping to ward off rivals, and they prefer to engage in direct confrontations rather than ambush tactics.

### The Cheetah

The cheetah, scientifically named *Acinonyx jubatus*, is the fastest land animal, found in the savannas and grasslands of Africa. Their slender bodies and long legs enable them to reach incredible speeds, making them expert hunters of small to medium-sized prey like gazelles and impalas. Cheetahs are known for their distinctive black tear marks under their eyes, which help reduce glare from the sun as they spot their next meal.

**Physical Stats:**

**Fighting Style:**
Cheetahs rely on their unparalleled speed and agility in the wild. They use a combination of stealth and quick bursts of acceleration to catch their prey, often closing the distance before launching a high-speed chase. In a confrontation, they would likely attempt to outmaneuver their opponent rather than engage in direct combat.
