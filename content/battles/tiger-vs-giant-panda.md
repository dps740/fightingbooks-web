## Meet the Fighters

### The Tiger

The tiger is a magnificent and powerful predator found in tropical forests, grasslands, and swamps across Asia. Known for their striking orange coat with black stripes, these big cats are not just beautiful; they are fierce hunters at the top of their food chain. Tigers are solitary creatures, relying on stealth and strength to catch their prey.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 220 to 660 pounds! 
- **Length / Height:** Up to 10 feet long including the tail! That's as long as a SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 30 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding CAR!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,000 PSI
- Sharp claws up to 3 inches long
- Strong legs for pouncing

**Fighting Style:**
Tigers are solitary hunters that rely on stealth and ambush tactics. They stalk their prey silently, using their camouflage to blend into the surroundings. When they are close enough, they pounce with explosive speed, using their powerful jaws and sharp claws to deliver a decisive blow.

### The Giant Panda

The giant panda, an adorable bear often seen in zoos, inhabits the misty mountain bamboo forests of central China. With its distinctive black-and-white fur, the panda is a symbol of conservation. However, despite their cuddly appearance, giant pandas are strong and capable fighters, especially when defending themselves.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 330 pounds — as heavy as an NFL lineman!
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long.
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 mph in short bursts — faster than most humans!

**Weapons:**
- Jaw muscles and teeth strong enough to CRUSH thick bamboo stalks — bite force of 292 PSI
- Sharp claws on powerful forearms for climbing and swiping 
- A special SIXTH FINGER (pseudo-thumb) made from a modified wrist bone for incredible grip strength!

**Fighting Style:**
While giant pandas primarily feed on bamboo, they are surprisingly agile and have strong defensive capabilities. If threatened, pandas can use their powerful forearms and sharp claws to swipe at potential threats. Their stocky build provides them with strength, and they can climb to escape danger quickly.
