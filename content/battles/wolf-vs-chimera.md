**Fighting Style:**

In a fight, a wolf would likely rely on its pack mentality, working together to flank and overpower its opponent.

### The Chimera

The chimera is a mythological creature, often depicted as having the head of a lion, the body of a goat, and a serpent for a tail. This fearsome beast hails from the mountains of Lycia in ancient Greece and is known to feast on anything from goats to humans, making it a terrifying figure in folklore.

In battle, it is likely to rely on its ferocity and mythical powers to dominate.
