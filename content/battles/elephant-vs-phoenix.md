## Meet the Fighters

### The Elephant

The African Elephant, scientifically known as *Loxodonta africana*, roams the vast savannas, forests, and grasslands of Africa. Known for its intelligence and strong social bonds, this gentle giant is revered as the largest land animal on Earth. With its massive size and incredible strength, the elephant is a powerful force in the animal kingdom.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 12,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 13 feet tall at the shoulder and 24 feet long! That's as long as TWO SCHOOL BUSES!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 25 mph! That's as fast as a running horse!

**Weapons:**
- Tusks, up to 10 feet long!
- Trunk
- Massive body

**Fighting Style:**
Elephants are known for their strength and intelligence in fights. They utilize their size to intimidate opponents and can deliver powerful blows with their tusks and trunk. While they often prefer to avoid conflict, they will fiercely defend themselves or their herd when threatened, charging directly at their adversaries.

### The Phoenix

The Phoenix is a legendary creature from mythology, often depicted as a bird with vibrant, fiery feathers. It inhabits fiery mountains and mystical realms, feeding on sunlight and flames. Known for its immortality and ability to be reborn from its ashes, the Phoenix is a symbol of renewal and strength. 

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approximately 200 pounds (like a large dog!)
- **Length / Height:** About 15 feet long! That's as long as a SEDAN!
- **Top Speed:** Can fly up to 100 mph! That's faster than a CAR on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Fiery talons (1 foot long)
- Blazing beak
- Fire breath

**Fighting Style:**
The Phoenix relies on its incredible speed and fiery abilities in combat. It can swoop down from the sky, using its talons and beak to attack. When threatened, it often unleashes fiery breath to scorch its enemies, creating a barrier of flames. Additionally, its ability to be reborn from ashes allows it to recover from defeats, making it a formidable opponent in any fight.
