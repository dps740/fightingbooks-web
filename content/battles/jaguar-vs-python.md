## Meet the Fighters

### The Jaguar

The jaguar, scientifically known as *Panthera onca*, is a magnificent big cat that roams the rainforests, swamps, and grasslands of Central and South America. **Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
Jaguars are solitary hunters that rely on stealth and surprise. They often ambush their prey, using their incredible speed and strength to deliver a swift, fatal bite. They are known to attack from trees or the water, making them unpredictable and highly effective hunters.

### The Python

The green python, or *Morelia viridis*, is a stunning snake that thrives in the tropical rainforests of New Guinea and Australia. With its vibrant green scales that help it blend into the leaves, this snake is an expert at camouflage and patience.

- **Weight:** Up to 30 pounds (approx.)
- **Length / Height:** Up to 13 feet long!
- **Top Speed:** Can strike at speeds of 3 mph! That's like running as fast as a slow jogger!
- **Bite Force / Strength:** Not specified (approx.)

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**
Pythons are ambush predators that rely on their ability to conceal themselves in the foliage. They strike quickly to catch their prey off guard, using their powerful muscles to constrict and suffocate their victims. Green pythons are known for their ability to climb trees, giving them an advantage in their arboreal habitat.
