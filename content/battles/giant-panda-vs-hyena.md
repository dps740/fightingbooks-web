The Spotted Hyena, scientifically known as *Crocuta crocuta*, is a highly social animal found in the savannas, grasslands, and woodlands of Africa. Known for their distinctive laugh and pack mentality, these animals weigh around 110 pounds and are often underrated due to their scavenging habits. However, they're fierce hunters with a reputation for being one of Africa's top predators.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 110 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.5 feet long and 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**

While they can fight alone, they often rely on teamwork to overwhelm their foes, using their sharp teeth and powerful jaws to inflict damage.
