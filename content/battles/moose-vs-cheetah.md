## Meet the Fighters

### The Moose

The moose, or *Alces alces*, is a majestic herbivore found in the forests and wetlands of North America, Europe, and Asia. Known for their impressive antlers and large size, these gentle giants are usually solitary but can be quite fierce when threatened. With their long legs and massive bodies, moose are well-adapted to their environments, often seen grazing on leaves, twigs, and even aquatic plants.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 1,500 pounds!
- **Length / Height:** 6 to 7.5 feet tall at the shoulder.
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 35 mph! That's as fast as a speeding CAR!

**Weapons:**
- Antlers up to 6 feet wide!
- Strong legs
- Kicking power

**Fighting Style:**
Moose typically rely on their size and strength to fend off predators. When threatened, they can use their powerful legs to kick or charge at an opponent. Their impressive antlers can also be used for defense, swinging them like a weapon when necessary. Although moose are mostly solitary, they can become aggressive if they feel cornered or if their young are at risk.

### The Cheetah

The cheetah, or *Acinonyx jubatus*, is the fastest land animal, roaming the savannas and grasslands of Africa. Known for their slender bodies, distinctive black tear stripes, and incredible speed, cheetahs are skilled hunters. They primarily feed on small to medium-sized animals, such as gazelles and impalas, requiring daily meals to maintain their energy levels.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 110 to 140 pounds.
- **Length / Height:** Up to 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder and 3.5 to 4.5 feet long.
- **Top Speed:** Can reach speeds of 60 to 70 mph! That's faster than a school bus on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Sharp claws (up to 2 inches long)
- Powerful jaws
- Speedy legs

**Fighting Style:**
Cheetahs are built for speed and quick bursts of energy. They typically stalk their prey before launching a high-speed chase, reaching their top speeds to catch unsuspecting animals. In a fight, they rely on their agility, using quick turns and bursts to evade larger opponents. If necessary, they can use their sharp claws and powerful jaws to defend themselves.
