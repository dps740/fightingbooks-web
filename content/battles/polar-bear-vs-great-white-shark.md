## Meet the Fighters

### The Polar Bear

The polar bear, known scientifically as *Ursus maritimus*, is a magnificent beast that roams the icy Arctic regions, primarily on sea ice. With its thick white fur and immense size, this carnivorous giant is a master hunter, primarily preying on seals, fish, and sometimes even birds. Polar bears are not just iconic symbols of the Arctic; they are formidable predators that can weigh up to 1,500 pounds!

**Fighting Style:**

Their fighting style is aggressive, utilizing their sharp claws and powerful jaws to overpower opponents.

### The Great White Shark

The great white shark, or *Carcharodon carcharias*, is one of the ocean’s top predators and can be found in coastal waters worldwide. Known for their impressive size and razor-sharp teeth, these carnivorous fish can grow up to 20 feet long and weigh over 2,400 pounds! Great whites are not just fearsome hunters; they are also incredibly agile swimmers, capable of speeds that can leave their prey in the dust.

**Fighting Style:**

They use their superior speed and strength to launch surprise attacks from below, delivering powerful bites that can incapacitate their target. Their fighting style is swift and deadly, making them one of the ocean's most effective hunters.
