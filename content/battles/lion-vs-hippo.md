When attacking, a lion will stalk its target, using the element of surprise to pounce at high speed. Their sharp claws and powerful jaws make for deadly weapons when they engage in a fight.

The common hippopotamus, or *Hippopotamus amphibius*, is a creature of the rivers and lakes of Africa. Known for its massive size and aggressive nature, the hippo spends most of its day submerged in water to keep cool, but don't let that fool you — they can be incredibly dangerous on land.
