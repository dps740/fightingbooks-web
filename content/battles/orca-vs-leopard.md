## Meet the Fighters

### The Orca

The orca, scientifically known as *Orcinus orca*, is a magnificent marine mammal found in oceans worldwide, especially in colder waters. With its sleek black and white coloration, the orca has earned a reputation as a top predator, feared and admired by all who encounter it.

- **Length / Height:** Up to 32 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can swim up to 34.5 mph
- **Bite Force / Strength:** (Not specified)

**Weapons:**

**Fighting Style:**

They can employ various tactics, such as creating waves to knock seals off ice floes or using their speed to chase down fish. Their intelligence allows them to adapt their strategies based on the situation, making them formidable opponents in the water.

### The Leopard

The leopard, or *Panthera pardus*, is a stunning and adaptable big cat found in savannas, forests, and grasslands across Africa and parts of Asia. Known for its beautiful spotted fur and incredible climbing ability, the leopard is a skilled hunter that can take down prey much larger than itself.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Between 66 to 176 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.25 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 36 mph
- **Bite Force / Strength:** (Not specified)

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 2 inches long)
- Sharp claws
- Agile body

**Fighting Style:**
Leopards rely on stealth and surprise, often stalking their prey before launching a quick and powerful attack. They are solitary hunters and can climb trees to escape threats or store their kills away from scavengers. Their agility and strength make them excellent fighters in their terrestrial habitat.
