**Fighting Style:**

The Spotted Hyena, scientifically known as *Crocuta crocuta*, is a social and highly adaptable predator found in the savannas, grasslands, and woodlands of Africa. **Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 190 pounds
- **Length / Height:** 5.5 to 6.5 feet long! That's about as long as a small sedan!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 37 miles per hour!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,100 PSI!
- Sharp claws
- Strong legs for running

**Fighting Style:**
Hyenas are known for their pack behavior, often hunting in groups to take down larger prey. They use their strength, stamina, and teamwork to outmaneuver and overwhelm their targets. When it comes to fighting, they can be aggressive and are not afraid to defend their territory or food from other predators.
