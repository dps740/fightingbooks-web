## Meet the Fighters

### The Gorilla

Gorillas are the largest primates on Earth, residing primarily in the tropical forests of Africa. Known for their gentle nature and strong social bonds, these magnificent creatures live in family groups led by a dominant male. Despite their reputation as peaceful giants, gorillas are incredibly powerful and can defend their territory when threatened.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 400 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet tall and 12 feet long when standing on all fours! That's as tall as a door!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding car in a school zone!

**Weapons:**
- Strong arms with a reach of 8 feet
- Powerful jaws
- Intimidating chest thump

**Fighting Style:**
Gorillas are known for their sheer strength and size. When threatened, they can use their powerful arms to strike or push back against an opponent. They often rely on intimidation, using their massive size and vocalizations to deter potential threats before resorting to physical confrontation.

### The Hyena

The spotted hyena is a fascinating carnivore that thrives in Africa's savannas, grasslands, and woodlands. Often misunderstood, these animals are intelligent and social, living in clans that can consist of up to 80 members! Known for their unique laughing calls, hyenas are skilled hunters and scavengers, using teamwork to bring down prey much larger than themselves.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 190 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder and 5.5 to 6.5 feet long! That's about as long as a small sedan!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 37 miles per hour! That's faster than a speeding car in a school zone!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 1,100 PSI!
- Sharp claws
- Strong legs for running

**Fighting Style:**
Hyenas are agile and strategic fighters. They often engage in pack behavior, using their numbers to their advantage. When attacking, they can surround their prey and employ their powerful jaws to deliver devastating bites. Their speed allows them to outmaneuver larger animals, making them formidable opponents in a fight.
