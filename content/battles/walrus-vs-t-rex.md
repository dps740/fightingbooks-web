### The T-Rex

The Tyrannosaurus rex, or T-Rex, is one of the most iconic dinosaurs that ever existed. Known for its massive skull, powerful jaws, and tiny arms, the T-Rex was a dominant force in its ecosystem, capable of taking down large prey with terrifying efficiency.
