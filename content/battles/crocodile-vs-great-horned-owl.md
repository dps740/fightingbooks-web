**Fighting Style:**

### The Great Horned Owl

The Great Horned Owl (Bubo virginianus) is a master of the night, found in forests, deserts, swamps, and even cities across North and South America. Known for its distinctive tufted "horns" and powerful presence, this owl is one of the most widespread in the Americas. With a voracious appetite, it hunts everything from small mammals to other birds, including hawks.

**Fighting Style:**
