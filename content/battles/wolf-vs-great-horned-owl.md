## Meet the Fighters

### The Wolf

The gray wolf, known scientifically as *Canis lupus*, is a highly social and intelligent carnivore that roams forests, tundras, and grasslands. These majestic creatures are renowned for their pack mentality, working together to hunt and protect their territory. With their keen sense of smell and powerful build, wolves are the ultimate symbol of wilderness.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 50-110 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.5 feet long (including tail) - ABOUT AS LONG AS A SCHOOL BUS!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 40 mph - FASTER THAN A SPRINTING HUMAN!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 1,500 PSI)
- Sharp claws
- Keen sense of smell

**Fighting Style:**
Wolves primarily hunt in packs, using teamwork and strategy to outsmart their prey. They can employ ambush tactics or chase down their targets with remarkable speed. When fighting, a wolf relies on its powerful jaws and sharp claws to subdue its opponent, often working in unison with pack members to increase their chances of success.

### The Great Horned Owl

The great horned owl, or *Bubo virginianus*, is one of the most formidable birds of prey in the Americas. Known for its striking appearance, with tufted "horns" that resemble cat ears, this owl inhabits forests, deserts, swamps, and even urban areas. Its versatility and fierce hunting skills make it a top predator in its environment.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 5.5 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Body is 2 feet long, wingspan up to 5 FEET - wider than most adults are tall!
- **Top Speed:** Can dive at 40 mph and fly at 40 mph - as fast as a car in a neighborhood!

**Weapons:**
- Talons grip with 300 PSI of crushing force — same as a large dog's BITE!
- Silent flight feathers with serrated edges eliminate ALL sound — prey never hears them coming!
- Can rotate head up to 270 degrees — almost a full circle — spotting prey in near-total darkness!

**Fighting Style:**
The great horned owl is a master of stealth. It hunts primarily at night, using its silent flight to surprise unsuspecting prey. With impressive agility and the ability to dive at high speeds, it can strike quickly and efficiently. When threatened, this owl doesn’t shy away from confrontation, fearlessly attacking animals far larger than itself.
