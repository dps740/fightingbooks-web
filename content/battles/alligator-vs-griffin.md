Alligators typically hunt alone, using their stealth and speed to catch fish, birds, and even larger animals like deer.

### The Griffin

The griffin, a legendary creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle, is a symbol of strength and courage in many cultures. With their majestic wings, they can maneuver quickly, making it difficult for ground-based predators to land a hit. They are known to be solitary hunters, relying on their keen eyesight to spot prey from high above.
