**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 9 tons
- **Length / Height:** 40 feet long and 12 feet tall
- **Top Speed:** 20 miles per hour

**Weapons:**
- Razor-sharp teeth (up to 12 inches long)

- Powerful jaws with crushing bite force

**Fighting Style:**
The T-Rex was primarily a solitary hunter, relying on ambush tactics to catch its prey off guard. With its keen sense of smell and quick speed, it would stalk its target, then pounce with deadly force. The T-Rex's massive jaws could crush bones, making it a fearsome opponent in any fight.

### The Brachiosaurus

Meet the Brachiosaurus, a gentle giant of the Jurassic period! This massive herbivore was known for its long neck and towering height, which allowed it to reach high into trees for tasty leaves. While not a predator, the Brachiosaurus was a magnificent sight, often found in forests and floodplains.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 62 tons
- **Length / Height:** 85 feet long and 40 feet tall
- **Top Speed:** 10 miles per hour

**Weapons:**
- Long neck (up to 30 feet)
- Strong legs for stability
- Massive body for defense

**Fighting Style:**
Though primarily a peaceful plant-eater, the Brachiosaurus could defend itself if threatened. Its sheer size alone acted as a deterrent to predators. If provoked, it could use its long neck to deliver powerful blows or trample attackers with its strong legs.
