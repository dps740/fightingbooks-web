- **Length / Height:** Up to 8 feet long! That's as long as a school bus!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 35 mph! That's faster than a galloping horse — NEVER try to outrun a grizzly!

**Weapons:**
- Powerful claws (up to 4 inches long)
- Strong jaws
- Massive body weight

**Fighting Style:**
Grizzly Bears are solitary fighters, relying on their brute strength and speed to tackle prey. They often use ambush tactics, striking swiftly with their powerful claws and jaws. Their size allows them to intimidate rivals, while their quick reflexes help them avoid attacks.

### The Spinosaurus

Enter the Spinosaurus, or *Spinosaurus aegypticus*, the massive carnivore of the ancient world! This dinosaur roamed the rivers and wetlands of North Africa, standing out with its unique sail-like structure on its back. Known for its size and ferocity, the Spinosaurus is often considered one of the largest carnivorous dinosaurs to have ever lived.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Estimated 7-20 tons
- **Length / Height:** Up to 50-59 feet long! That's like TWO school buses!
- **Top Speed:** Could move at about 12 mph! That's about as fast as a human sprinter — pretty quick for a 7-ton dinosaur!

**Weapons:**
- Teeth up to 12 inches long!
- Powerful claws
- Huge sail on its back

**Fighting Style:**
The Spinosaurus boasts a powerful bite and a strong tail, using these features to its advantage in battles. It often employs a tactic of swiping at its opponents with its sharp claws and teeth, overwhelming them with sheer force. Being an adept swimmer adds to its versatility, enabling it to strike from both land and water.
