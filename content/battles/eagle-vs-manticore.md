**Weapons:**

- Strong beak
- Keen eyesight

**Fighting Style:**
Bald Eagles are solitary hunters that primarily rely on their incredible vision and speed. Their powerful flight allows them to escape potential threats quickly.

The Manticore, a legendary creature from ancient Persian mythology, is a fearsome beast known for its terrifying appearance and ferocity. With the body of a lion, a human-like head, and a venomous scorpion tail, the Manticore is as intriguing as it is deadly, feasting on unsuspecting prey and terrorizing ancient landscapes.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Approximately 200-300 kg (440-660 lbs)
- **Length / Height:** About 3 meters (10 feet) long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 48 km/h (30 mph)

**Weapons:**
- Tail with venomous spikes (up to 91 cm / 3 feet long)
- Sharp claws
- Fangs

**Fighting Style:**
The Manticore is a formidable opponent that uses both brute strength and cunning tactics to catch its prey. It can mimic sounds to confuse animals, allowing it to sneak up undetected. When threatened, it can fiercely defend itself with its sharp claws and venomous tail.
