## Meet the Fighters

### The Gorilla

Gorillas are the largest primates on Earth, known for their incredible strength and gentle nature. Found in the tropical forests of Africa, these magnificent creatures are often regarded as peaceful giants, spending their days munching on leaves, stems, and fruit. Despite their imposing size, gorillas are incredibly intelligent and social animals.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 400 pounds! 
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet tall and 12 feet long when standing on all fours! That's as tall as a door!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 miles per hour! That's as fast as a speeding car in a school zone!

**Weapons:**
- Strong arms with a reach of 8 feet
- Powerful jaws
- Intimidating chest thump

**Fighting Style:**
Gorillas primarily rely on their brute strength and intelligence when it comes to fighting. They can stand their ground solo or defend their group against threats. In a confrontation, a gorilla might use its powerful arms to strike, while its chest thump serves to intimidate opponents.

### The Phoenix

The phoenix is a mythical creature known for its stunning beauty and fiery nature. Living in fiery mountains and mystical realms, this legendary bird is famous for its ability to be reborn from its own ashes. With its radiant feathers and powerful presence, the phoenix captures the imagination of many.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Could weigh as much as 200 pounds, like a large dog!
- **Length / Height:** About 15 feet long! That's as long as a SEDAN!
- **Top Speed:** Can fly up to 100 mph! That's faster than a CAR on the highway!

**Weapons:**
- Fiery talons (1 foot long)
- Blazing beak
- Fire breath

**Fighting Style:**
The phoenix fights with agility and speed, often using its ability to fly to gain an advantage. It can attack from the air with its fiery talons and beak while using its fire breath to create distance or deter attackers. The phoenix's immortality means it can return to battle, making it a formidable foe.
