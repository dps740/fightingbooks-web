## Meet the Fighters

### The Hammerhead Shark

The hammerhead shark, scientifically known as *Sphyrna mokarran*, is one of the most recognizable sharks in the ocean. With its unique hammer-shaped head, this powerful predator prowls warm tropical and temperate oceans worldwide, from vibrant coral reefs to the deep blue sea. Known for its impressive hunting skills, the hammerhead is a formidable foe in its watery domain.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 1,000 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 20 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 25 mph in bursts

**Weapons:**
- Wide hammer head that pins prey to the ocean floor
- Rows of razor-sharp serrated teeth that constantly regrow
- Incredible speed bursts for ambush attacks

**Fighting Style:**
Hammerhead sharks are lone hunters, often utilizing their unique head shape to pin down prey like stingrays and smaller fish. They rely on their speed and stealth, darting through the water to catch their next meal. These sharks can also travel in schools for safety, but when it comes to fighting, they are fierce solo warriors.

### The Cheetah

Meet the cheetah, scientifically known as *Acinonyx jubatus*, the fastest land animal on the planet! These agile cats roam the savannas and grasslands of Africa, where their incredible speed and sharp eyesight make them masterful hunters. With their sleek bodies and distinctive black tear markings, cheetahs are built for speed and agility.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 110 to 140 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 4.5 feet tall at the shoulder and 3.5 to 4.5 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 60 to 70 mph!

**Weapons:**
- Sharp claws (up to 2 inches long)
- Powerful jaws
- Speedy legs

**Fighting Style:**
Cheetahs are expert sprinters, capable of reaching speeds of 60 to 70 mph in just a few seconds. They rely on their incredible acceleration, using stealth and camouflage to get close to their prey before launching a rapid sprint. However, their bursts of speed can only be maintained for about 20-30 seconds, so they must choose their moments wisely.
