### The Giant Panda

The giant panda is native to the misty bamboo forests of central China, where it spends most of its time munching on bamboo. With their distinctive black-and-white coloring and adorable appearance, giant pandas are beloved worldwide. However, don’t let their cuddly looks fool you; they are powerful animals equipped to defend themselves.
