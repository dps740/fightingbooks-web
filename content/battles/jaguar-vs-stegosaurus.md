Jaguars are not just beautiful; they are fierce hunters, capable of taking down prey much larger than themselves.

**Fighting Style:**

### The Stegosaurus

The stegosaurus (Stegosaurus ungulatus) roamed the Earth during the late Jurassic period and is one of the most iconic dinosaurs. With its distinctive double row of large plates along its back and spiked tail, this massive herbivore was a sight to behold. Living in dry, open woodlands and plains, the stegosaurus was primarily a plant-eater, munching on leaves and ferns.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Around 5 tons (approximately 10,000 pounds)
- **Length / Height:** About 30 feet long! That's about as long as a school bus!
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 5 miles per hour! That's like a slow jog!

**Weapons:**
- Tail spikes (up to 2 feet long)
- Plates on back
- Strong legs

**Fighting Style:**
Stegosaurus primarily used its size and armor for defense. If threatened, it could swing its powerful tail, equipped with sharp spikes, to ward off attackers. Its defensive strategy was mostly about intimidation rather than aggression, relying on its massive size and striking features to deter predators.
