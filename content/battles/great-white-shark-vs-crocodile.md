### The Crocodile

The Saltwater Crocodile, or *Crocodylus porosus*, is found in coastal areas, rivers, and swamps from Southeast Asia to northern Australia. With a reputation as a fierce and cunning predator, the crocodile is a master of camouflage, blending seamlessly into its surroundings. When it comes to hunting, this reptile uses patience and brute force to catch its prey.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 2,200 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 23 feet long

- **Bite Force / Strength:** 3,700 pounds of bite force

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws with a bite force of 3,700 pounds

**Fighting Style:**
Crocodiles are known for their stealthy ambush tactics. They lie in wait, partially submerged, before lunging at their prey with lightning speed.
