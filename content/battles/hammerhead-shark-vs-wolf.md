### The Wolf

The Gray Wolf, known scientifically as *Canis lupus*, is a highly social and intelligent predator found in forests, tundras, and grasslands. Renowned for their teamwork and hunting strategies, wolves are fierce competitors and are often seen hunting in packs, making them even more formidable against larger prey.

**Fighting Style:**
