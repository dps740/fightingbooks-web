### The Great Horned Owl

The great horned owl, or *Bubo virginianus*, is a fascinating predator found in forests, deserts, swamps, and even bustling cities throughout North and South America. With their distinctive tufts resembling horns, these owls are often referred to as "tigers of the sky." They are known for their adaptability and fierce hunting skills, making them one of the most widespread owls in the Americas.

**Fighting Style:**
Great horned owls are master ambush predators. They rely on their silent flight and excellent night vision to hunt. Though they are smaller than lions, their agility and stealth are their greatest advantages in a fight.
