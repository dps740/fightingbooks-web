### The Giant Panda

The giant panda, or *Ailuropoda melanoleuca*, is a beloved symbol of wildlife conservation, found in the misty bamboo forests of central China. Often perceived as cuddly and cute, pandas are surprisingly powerful animals. Weighing in at around 330 pounds, they spend most of their day munching on bamboo—up to 84 pounds daily! Despite their gentle demeanor, pandas possess impressive strength and agility.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 330 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 mph
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 292 PSI

**Weapons:**

- A special sixth finger (pseudo-thumb) for incredible grip strength

**Fighting Style:**
While giant pandas are not typically aggressive, they can defend themselves if threatened. In a fight, a panda might use its strong bite and swiping motions to fend off an attacker, making it a surprisingly capable opponent.
