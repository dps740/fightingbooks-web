The common octopus, known scientifically as *Octopus vulgaris*, is a fascinating marine animal found in oceans and seas around the world. Renowned for its intelligence and unique abilities, this creature can change color and texture to blend seamlessly with its surroundings. The octopus is a master of escape, using its agility and clever tactics to outsmart predators and capture prey.

They can also change their color and texture to blend into their environment, making it hard for predators to spot them. While they can use their beak to bite, their primary strategy involves using stealth and agility to outmaneuver opponents.
