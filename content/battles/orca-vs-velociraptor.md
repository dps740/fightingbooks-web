## Meet the Fighters

### The Orca

The orca, scientifically known as *Orcinus orca*, is a powerful marine mammal found in oceans worldwide, especially in colder waters. Known as the "killer whale," the orca is actually the largest member of the dolphin family, and it has earned a reputation as one of the ocean's top predators. With its striking black and white coloration, the orca is not just beautiful; it's also incredibly intelligent and social.

### The Velociraptor

The velociraptor, scientifically known as *Velociraptor mongoliensis*, was a small yet fierce dinosaur that roamed the dry, open areas of what is now Mongolia during the Late Cretaceous period. Although often depicted as a larger creature in movies, velociraptors were actually about the size of a turkey, making them agile hunters. With their sharp claws and teeth, these cunning predators hunted in packs, showcasing their intelligence and teamwork.
