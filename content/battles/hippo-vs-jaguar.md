### The Jaguar

The jaguar, scientifically known as Panthera onca, is a stunning big cat native to the rainforests, swamps, and grasslands of Central and South America. Renowned for their beautiful rosettes and incredible strength, jaguars are apex predators, capable of taking down prey much larger than themselves. Their stealth and hunting prowess make them one of the most dangerous cats in the wild.

In a fight, they would likely use their agility and quick reflexes to dodge attacks while delivering precise strikes.
