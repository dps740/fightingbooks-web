### The Triceratops

The Triceratops, scientifically known as *Triceratops horridus*, roamed the open plains and forests of North America during the late Cretaceous period. This massive herbivore was famous for its three distinct facial horns and bony frill, which provided both defense and display. Triceratops were not just gentle giants; they were equipped to defend themselves against predators with their powerful build and impressive weaponry.
