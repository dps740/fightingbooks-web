## Meet the Fighters

The giant panda, known scientifically as *Ailuropoda melanoleuca*, is a bear native to the misty mountain bamboo forests of central China. These adorable creatures are famous for their black-and-white fur and their love for munching on bamboo. Surprisingly, despite their cute appearance, giant pandas are powerful animals with impressive physical capabilities!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 330 pounds
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 mph in short bursts
- **Bite Force / Strength:** 292 PSI

**Fighting Style:**
Giant pandas may not be natural fighters, but they have strong forearms and sharp claws that can swipe at opponents if threatened. They also have the ability to climb trees swiftly, allowing them to escape from danger. While they typically prefer a peaceful life of eating bamboo, they can definitely hold their own if provoked!

### The Stegosaurus

The stegosaurus, scientifically named *Stegosaurus ungulatus*, roamed the Earth during the Late Jurassic period. This iconic dinosaur is well-known for its large, bony plates along its back and its spiked tail, known as the thagomizer. Stegosaurus lived in dry, open woodlands and plains, munching on plants, leaves, and ferns.
