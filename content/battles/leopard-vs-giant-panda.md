## Meet the Fighters

### The Leopard

The leopard, a member of the big cat family, is a stunning creature known for its beautiful spotted coat and elusive nature. Found in savannas, forests, and grasslands across Africa and parts of Asia, leopards are skilled hunters that can adapt to a variety of habitats. Their reputation as stealthy stalkers has made them one of the top predators in their environment.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 66 to 176 pounds (about as heavy as a large dog!)
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6.25 feet long (that's like a small school bus!)
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 36 mph (faster than a galloping horse!)

**Weapons:**
- Powerful jaws (up to 2 inches long)
- Sharp claws
- Agile body

**Fighting Style:**
Leopards are solitary hunters that rely on stealth and ambush tactics to catch their prey. They often stalk through the underbrush, using their spotted fur for camouflage, before launching a surprise attack. Their agility allows them to make quick turns and powerful leaps, making them formidable opponents in a fight.

### The Giant Panda

The Giant Panda, native to the misty mountain bamboo forests of central China, is an iconic bear known for its distinctive black-and-white markings. They thrive in the high elevations of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu provinces, where they spend most of their time munching on bamboo. Despite their cuddly appearance, giant pandas are surprisingly powerful animals.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Up to 330 pounds (as heavy as an NFL lineman!)
- **Length / Height:** Up to 6 feet long
- **Top Speed:** Can run up to 20 mph in short bursts

**Weapons:**
- Jaw muscles and teeth strong enough to crush thick bamboo stalks (bite force of 292 PSI)
- Sharp claws on powerful forearms for climbing and swiping
- A special sixth finger (pseudo-thumb) for incredible grip strength

**Fighting Style:**
Giant pandas may seem docile, but they are strong fighters when necessary. They can stand their ground using their massive build and powerful jaws. While they primarily eat bamboo, their sharp claws and strength allow them to defend themselves fiercely against any threat.
