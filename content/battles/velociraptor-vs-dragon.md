## Meet the Fighters

### The Velociraptor

The Velociraptor, scientifically known as *Velociraptor mongoliensis*, roamed the dry, open plains and deserts of what is now Mongolia around 75 million years ago. Despite its relatively small size, this dinosaur earned a fearsome reputation as a clever predator, capable of hunting in packs. Think of it as a highly skilled assassin of the dinosaur world!

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 33 pounds
- **Length / Height:** About 6.5 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 24 mph

**Weapons:**
- Clawed toes (up to 3 inches long)
- Sharp teeth
- Speed and agility

**Fighting Style:**
The Velociraptor is known for its speed and agility. It often uses ambush tactics, relying on its sharp claws and quick reflexes to attack its prey. Although it can fight solo, it is most effective when hunting in packs, coordinating attacks to overwhelm larger animals.

### The Dragon

Enter the Dragon, specifically the fire-breathing variety, scientifically dubbed *Draco Ignis*. This majestic creature can be found soaring the skies from its mountainous lairs or hiding in rocky caves. With legends speaking of its size and power, the Dragon is often depicted as a fierce guardian, capable of taking on almost any adversary with its fiery breath and formidable presence.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** 1,500 pounds
- **Length / Height:** 30 feet long
- **Top Speed:** 60 mph

**Weapons:**
- Fire Breath (up to 1,000°F)
- Sharp claws (2 feet long)
- Tail swipe (5 feet wide)

**Fighting Style:**
The Dragon’s fighting style is a blend of aerial agility and brute force. It can unleash a torrent of flame from its mouth, creating a fiery barrier against attackers. With its sharp claws and powerful tail, it can engage in both aerial and ground combat, making it a formidable opponent from any angle.
