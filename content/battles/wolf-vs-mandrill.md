Wolves are tactical fighters, often utilizing teamwork and strategy when hunting. They will often work together to surround and ambush their prey. When fighting, they are known to use their speed and agility to dart in and out, delivering quick bites while avoiding counterattacks.

They are social animals, living in troops that can number up to 800 individuals, showcasing their intelligence and complex social structures.
