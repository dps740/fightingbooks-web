### The Pteranodon

The pteranodon, scientifically known as *Pteranodon longiceps*, was one of the largest flying reptiles of its time, boasting an impressive wingspan of up to 33 feet! This remarkable creature lived during the Late Cretaceous period, around 70 million years ago, and ruled the skies above coastal areas. Although not a dinosaur, the pteranodon was a master of aerial acrobatics and fishing, diving into the ocean to catch its meals.

**Physical Stats:**
- **Weight:** Estimated at around 50 to 80 pounds (approx.)

**Fighting Style:**
