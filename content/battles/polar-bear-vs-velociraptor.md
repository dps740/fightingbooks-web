## Meet the Fighters

### The Polar Bear

The polar bear, known scientifically as *Ursus maritimus*, is the largest land carnivore found in the Arctic regions, primarily on sea ice. With its impressive size and strength, it's a true king of the cold, known for its ability to hunt seals and other marine animals. Its thick fur and layer of blubber keep it warm while it prowls its icy domain, making it a fearsome predator.

- **Weight:** Up to 1,500 pounds

**Fighting Style:**

In a fight, they would aim to use their weight and strength to overpower their opponent, grappling and biting with their powerful jaws.
