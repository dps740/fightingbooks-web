**Q: How is this different from just using ChatGPT?**
A: It's formatted as an actual children's book with illustrations, proper structure (intro, stats, battle, winner), and a flip-book reader. ChatGPT gives you a wall of text. This gives you something you can read with your kid or print and keep.

**Q: Any plans for more features?**
A: We're working on:
- Tournament mode (8-animal bracket)
- Choose Your Own Adventure battles
- Classroom bulk accounts for teachers
- Print-on-demand physical books

**Q: Is the science accurate?**
A: We research real data â€” size, weight, habitat, diet, weapons/defenses. The battle outcome is speculative but grounded in actual biology. We cite the same kind of facts Jerry Pallotta uses.

**Q: Legal concerns with the "Who Would Win" comparison?**
