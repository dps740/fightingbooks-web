# FightingBooks Content Calendar

## SEO Strategy Overview

Each article targets specific keywords with search volume. Articles serve two purposes:
1. Capture organic traffic
2. Funnel readers to create their own battles

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every page should link to:
1. The cornerstone "Complete Guide" article
2. 3-5 related battle pages
3. The homepage/generator
4. Amazon affiliate links for Jerry's books

This creates a web that:
- Keeps visitors on site longer
- Passes SEO juice between pages
- Funnels everyone to the generator
