# Fan Site Opportunities Analysis

## The Playbook

Look for:
1. **Popular book series** (Amazon bestsellers, 10+ books)
2. **Active search volume** (people looking for more)
3. **No official tool/generator** exists
4. **Replicable with AI** (can generate similar content)
5. **Clear monetization** (affiliate links, small fees, ads)
